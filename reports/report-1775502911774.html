2. Discussion Record 貳、 會議討論內容

Item 1: Foxlink Organizational Transformation & Global Strategy

議題一：正崴 (Foxlink) 企業組織轉型與全球戰略佈局

Foxlink is undergoing a profound organizational transformation, integrating previously siloed product lines into the CBG (Consumer Business Group) to enhance strategic execution.

正崴正進行深刻的組織變革，將以往孤島式 (Siloed) 的產品線運作整合為 CBG (Consumer Business Group) 事業群，以提升戰略執行效率。

  • Client-Centric Mindset: President Thomas brings multinational management experience from Google (as GSM), empowering Foxlink to precisely anticipate key account requirements for supply chain transparency and resilience.
  • 客戶端戰略思維： 總裁 Thomas 具備在 Google 擔任全球供應經理 (GSM) 的跨國經驗，賦予正崴「以大客戶視角思考」的戰略能力，能精準預判三星等巨擘對供應鏈透明度與彈性的要求。
  • Global "Non-China" Footprint: Foxlink operates deeply in North America, Vietnam, Taiwan, and China. The "Made in USA" Dallas plant successfully supports top-tier clients (Apple, Meta, Google, Nvidia), proving Foxlink's ability to build complex, automated lines outside China.
  • 全球與「非中」生產實力： 正崴在北美、越南、台灣及中國均有深度佈局。其達拉斯廠的「Made in USA」計畫已成功支援 Apple、Meta 等頂級客戶，為「Make in India」提供了可複製的自動化成功模型。
  • Tirupati Base Status: The India Tirupati plant (built ground-up) has been operational for 6-7 years, equipped with mature SMT and cable assembly capacity, forming a robust industrial cluster with nearby suppliers (e.g., Sunny Optical).
  • 印度 Tirupati 基地現狀： 該廠已營運 6-7 年，屬自建全新廠房，具備成熟的 SMT 與線纜組裝產能，並與周邊供應鏈 (如 Sunny Optical) 形成良好的產業聚落。

Item 2: Samsung India Sourcing Strategy & Localization Needs

議題二：三星印度戰略採購策略與在地化需求

Samsung's Strategic Sourcing Group (SSG), operating under the Singapore IPC, handles supply chain development for both the Noida (Mobile) and Chennai (Consumer Electronics) factories. They highlighted significant supply chain vulnerabilities in the current Indian FPC market.

隸屬於新加坡 IPC 的三星印度戰略採購團隊 (SSG) 負責諾伊達 (移動設備) 與清奈 (消費電子) 兩大工廠的供應鏈開發。他們指出當前印度 FPC 市場存在嚴重的供應鏈脆弱性。

  • Market Monopoly: FPC supply is currently monopolized by a single Apple supplier in Chennai, who shows "zero interest in servicing non-Apple clients."
  • 市場壟斷現況： 目前關鍵供應來源被一家位於清奈的 Apple 供應商獨佔，且該供應商「對服務非 Apple 客戶毫無興趣」。
  • Strategic Gap: Samsung urgently requires a globally capable tech partner to break this monopoly, mitigate single-source risks, and provide sustainable technical innovation.
  • 戰略缺口： 三星亟需一個具備全球視野與技術實力的長期戰略夥伴來打破此壟斷格局，規避單一來源風險並獲取持續的技術支援。
  • Core Value: Samsung is explicitly looking for a "Samsung Family" member for a long-term partnership, not just a short-term OEM arrangement.
  • 核心價值觀： 三星強調尋找的是 「Samsung Family」 長期戰略成員，而非短期的代工關係，這與 Foxlink 當前的轉型目標高度契合。

Item 3: FPC Partnership Intent & Logistics Bottlenecks

議題三：FPC 合作意向與物流瓶頸辯論

Discussions focused on the practicalities of localized FPC production, specifically the integration of Front-end and Back-end (SMT) processes.

雙方務實探討了 FPC 在地化生產的技術細節，特別是前段 (Front-end) 與後段 (SMT) 工藝的整合問題。

Location & Logistics Risk: Samsung recommends setting up in North India (e.g., Noida). They strongly warned that for critical components like FPCs, a logistics distance exceeding 100 km dramatically increases supply chain risks. Foxlink must carefully weigh utilizing the existing Tirupati base vs. proximity to Noida.

選址與物流風險： 三星建議 Foxlink 考慮在北印度 (如諾伊達) 設廠。三星強烈警示：對於 FPC 這類關鍵組件，若物流距離超過 100 公里，將大幅增加供應鏈風險。 Foxlink 需權衡 Tirupati 現有基地與靠近諾伊達廠之間的效益。

Brand Alignment Note: President Thomas deliberately utilized his Samsung Fold/Flip device during the meeting, visibly demonstrating strong brand alignment and partnership intent.

品牌認同備註：Thomas 總裁在會中特意展示其日常使用的 Samsung Fold/Flip 摺疊機，展現了對三星品牌的高度認同與強烈合作意向。

Item 4: Competitive Dynamics & Foxlink's Negotiation Stance

議題四：市場競爭動態與 Foxlink 談判底線

Foxlink is currently operating in a strong "Seller's Market" within the Indian electronics ecosystem (highlighted by a meeting with Vivo earlier the same day). Foxlink noted the immense pressure created by Apple's strategy (demanding 25% capacity transfer to India, quarterly 20% price cuts, and exclusive capacity).

Foxlink 目前在印度電子製造生態中處於極具優勢的賣方市場地位 (會中提及今日上午剛與 Vivo 完成會談)。Foxlink 也指出 Apple 策略帶來的巨大壓力 (要求 25% 產能轉移印度、每季 20% 降價及產能獨佔)。

Key Negotiation Pillar: No Investment = No Exclusivity. Foxlink made it explicitly clear: Unless clients are willing to provide dedicated CapEx investment ("write a check"), Foxlink will firmly maintain a non-exclusive capacity principle. Foxlink aims to build a diversified portfolio (Samsung, SpaceX, Chinese brands) to maximize equipment utilization and de-risk single-client exposure.

戰略談判支柱：不投資即無獨佔。 Foxlink 明確表態：除非客戶端提供專項投資 (Write a check)，否則 Foxlink 將堅守非排他性 (Non-exclusive) 原則。Foxlink 傾向建立包含 Samsung、SpaceX 及中國品牌的多樣化客戶組合，以極大化稼動率並分散單一客戶風險。
