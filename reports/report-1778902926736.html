5. 銷售與通路佈局

Hybrid Model & Digital Reach

全通路佈局與數位化

  • B2B Enterprise: Handled directly via Account Managers & tied with IT System Integrators (TCS, Wipro, HCL) for data center deals.
  • B2B 企業級： 仰賴客戶經理與系統整合商 (TCS, Wipro, HCL) 對接伺服器與資料中心業務。
  • Retail/LFRs: Over 600+ Dell Exclusive Stores in Tier 2/3 cities. Heavy presence in Reliance Digital, Croma.
  • 實體經銷： 全印擁有 600+ Dell 專賣店，深度進駐 Reliance Digital 等大型 3C 賣場。
  • E-commerce/CTOM: Top 3 seller on Amazon/Flipkart. Website offers Configure-to-Order (BTO) dispatching direct from factory.
  • 電商與官網： 官網支援「客製化接單生產(CTOM)」，由工廠直發；Amazon/Flipkart 常年霸榜前三。

Supply Chain & Logistics Logic

供應鏈物流邏輯

  • Just-in-Time & VMI: Vendors must be capable of delivering parts to Chennai bonded warehouse or directly VMI to Sriperumbudur.
  • VMI 與保稅倉： 零組件供應商需具備運送至 Chennai 保稅倉或直接 VMI 至 Sriperumbudur 廠區的能力。
  • Build-to-Order Agility: Supply chain designed for highly flexible and rapid turnover to meet CTOM line requirements.
  • 高彈性流轉： 產線高度依賴彈性物流，以滿足 BTO (客製化生產) 的快速流轉需求。
