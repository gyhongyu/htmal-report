2. Discussion Record 貳、 會議討論內容

Item 1: Foxlink's Strategic Objective

議題一：正崴 (Foxlink) 戰略目標說明

Foxlink explicitly stated that the immediate priority is to secure a Letter of Intent (LOI) from a Tier-1 Indian corporation (e.g., Tata, Reliance). This LOI is the mandatory condition to validate the "Made in India" strategy and unlock further group investment.

我方明確表示，目前的當務之急是取得印度一級企業（如 Tata、Reliance）的意向書 (LOI)。此 LOI 是驗證「Made in India」戰略並啟動集團後續投資的必要條件。

Item 2: Paulbert's Proposal - Turnkey Services

議題二：Paulbert 提案 - 一站式建廠服務

Dr. Singh presented Paulbert Talenting's end-to-end services to support Foxlink's entry:

Dr. Singh 介紹了 Paulbert Talenting 可提供的端對端建廠支援服務：

  • Subsidies: Assisting in securing 50% government subsidy on land and 5 years of free electricity.
  • 補貼申請： 協助爭取 50% 的政府土地補貼以及 5 年免費電力供應。
  • HR Compliance: Offering a service to "Pay Indian employee wages without local entity" to reduce initial operational risk.
  • 人資合規： 提供「無實體代付薪資」服務，降低初期營運風險。
  • Infrastructure: Land acquisition support in Gurgaon, Manesar, Noida, and Faridabad.
  • 基礎設施： 支援古爾岡、馬尼薩爾、諾伊達及法里達巴德的土地取得。

Item 3: Strategic Access - The MACE Connection

議題三：關鍵人脈 - MACE (Maruti Suzuki Centre for Excellence)

Dr. Singh identified that the most viable path to an LOI is through MACE, which controls audits for 1,200+ suppliers.

Dr. Singh 指出，取得 LOI 最可行的路徑是通過 MACE，該機構掌控了 1200 多家供應商的稽核權。

Key Contact: Mr. Prashant (Director, MACE). Dr. Singh confirmed he is a close personal friend.

關鍵聯絡人： Mr. Prashant (MACE 總監)。Dr. Singh 確認與其有深厚私人交情。

Dr. Singh mentioned Mr. Prashant has a standing request: "Take some auto big companies joint venture with us."

Dr. Singh 提及 Mr. Prashant 曾明確表示：「帶一些大型汽車公司來跟我們談合資。」
