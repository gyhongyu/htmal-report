1. Executive Summary: The Re-engagement Strategy

壹、 執行摘要：重啟合作戰略

Bolt.Earth (Revosauto Tech) is aggressively pivoting from L2/L3 AC chargers to high-power DC Fast Chargers (up to 240kW) following their Series B ($20M) and Series A+ ($5M) funding rounds. While they recently inaugurated a "Testing & Assembly" facility in Bangalore (2025), their paid-up capital (INR 1.4L) remains low, indicating they are NOT building a heavy manufacturing asset base. They need reliable partners to handle the volume production of these complex DC chargers to meet their FY27 IPO targets.

Bolt.Earth (Revosauto Tech) 在完成 Series B (2000萬美元) 與 Series A+ (500萬美元) 融資後，正積極從慢充 (AC) 轉向高功率直流快充 (DC Fast Charging, 240kW)。雖然他們 2025 年在班加羅爾啟用了新的「測試與組裝中心」，但其註冊資本 (INR 1.4 Lakhs) 極低，顯示其核心仍是輕資產運營。為了達成 2027 年 IPO 的營收目標，他們無法完全自製，極需具備規模量產能力的 EMS 夥伴來承接複雜的 DC 快充訂單。

Strategic Pitch: 核心話術：

"Raghav, we supported your Lite series growth. Now, as you target the IPO in 2027 with high-stakes DC Fast Chargers, let Foxlink handle the complex Turnkey manufacturing. Your new Bangalore facility is great for NPI, but our Chennai plant is built for the Scale you need."

「Raghav, 過去我們支持了 Bolt Lite 的成長。現在貴司目標 2027 IPO 並全力進攻高難度的 DC 快充，正崴能提供完整的 Turnkey 製造服務。貴司新的班加羅爾工廠適合做 NPI (新品導入)，但要達到 IPO 所需的規模量產，正崴的欽奈工廠才是最佳後盾。」
