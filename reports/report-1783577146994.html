5. Sales & Channel Strategy

5. 銷售與通路佈局

B2G Dominant Model

B2G 政府採購絕對主導

  • Government/Military Tenders (>90%): Highly structured procurement via Indian Navy, Army, Coast Guard, DRDO, and DPSUs (Defence Public Sector Undertakings).
  • 政府與軍方採購 (>90%): 作為高機密性的國防安全供應商，絕大部分營收來自軍方、海岸警衛隊及 DRDO、DPSUs 的公開與定向招標。
  • B2B Enterprise Direct: Providing automated retrofitting (Existing Craft Conversion) and real-time mapping data feeds to Adani Ports, ONGC marine setups, and disaster authorities.
  • B2B 行業直銷模式: 面向大型商業港口 (如 Adani 港口)、海上油氣田與防災管理局，提供無人艇數據監測服務和現有船隻的自主化改裝。
  • Retail E-Commerce (Zero): Defense-grade tech. Absolutely no distribution presence on Amazon India, Moglix or Industrybuying. Official portal (https://www.sagardefence.com/) acts as the only verified B2B query point.
  • 零售電商為「無」: 由於屬於受控的國防級設備，在大眾及工業電商平台上無任何銷售，唯一有效的線上商務對接窗口為其安全官方直連網址。

Logistics & Compliance Logic

供應鏈與交付物流邏輯

  • Closed Loop Logistics: "Pune Assembly -> Systems Calibration -> On-field Trial -> Dispatch to Base". Partners (like Foxlink) must sign strict non-disclosure agreements (NDAs).
  • 高安全性封閉物流: 採用「Pune 廠裝配 -> 原型機聯調 -> 海上/現地實測 (Field Trials) -> 交付軍方基地」的封閉安全模式，需簽署嚴格保密協定。
  • Military Standards: Quality operations must comply with high compliance setups (AS9100, ISO 9001:2015, or equivalents) to qualify for active defense tender sub-contracts.
  • 軍規級品管要求: 供應鏈合作夥伴需通過其嚴格的保密協定與國防級品管體系（如 AS9100 航太與國防品質管理認證）。
