Channel Layout & Direct Design-In

通路佈局與 B2B 技術嵌入 (Direct Design-In)

  • Direct Sourcing to OEMs: Micro-bearings, stepper motors, and cooling fans are customized and directly supplied to consumer electronic giants (such as LG, Samsung) and home appliance leaders (like Blue Star).
  • 對消費電子 (如 LG, Samsung) 和空調巨頭 (如 Blue Star) 採取直接供貨技術嵌入模式。
  • Automotive Tier-1 & Tier-2: For vehicle components (U-Shin, MAS), products are directly embedded into Indian automobile manufacturers' supply networks.
  • 針對汽車電子 (U-Shin, MAS)，直接作為 Tier-1 或 Tier-2 深度嵌入整車大廠供應鏈。
  • Industrial Distributor Networks: For common industrial replacement parts, an authorized network of regional distributors spans major hubs like Delhi, Mumbai, and Chennai.
  • 在德里、孟買、清奈等工業聚落設立授權工業軸承與電機分銷網路，鎖定通用工業及維修售後市場。
  • Pure B2B Digital Presence: Web interaction is strictly on B2B setups. Only official portal http://www.minebeamitsumi.co.in/ and verified ExportersIndia catalog ExportersIndia Store are used. Do not engage in B2C platforms (no Amazon.in store).
  • 數位管道限於 B2B。官方直連網址為 http://www.minebeamitsumi.co.in/，並設有 ExportersIndia 驗證之 B2B 展示商鋪。不參與 B2C 零售 (無 Amazon.in 直營店)。

Supply Chain Logistics Logic

供應鏈物流與倉儲邏輯

  • Strict JIT Delivery Model: High precision components dispatch directly from Bangalore units or strategically placed transit hubs near automotive manufacturing clusters.
  • 嚴格客製化 JIT 配送： 貨物直接從班加羅爾工廠或清奈、浦那的中央中轉倉發出，與整車廠客戶拉動式看板生產對接。
  • Vendor Warehousing Requirements: Demands localized warehouse partners near Gurugram, Pune, and Chennai to offset supply line fluctuations.
  • 供應商配套要求： 要求零組件外協夥伴具備高反應速度及穩定的在地倉儲備料，確保無縫對接。
