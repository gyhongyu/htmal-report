0. Executive Summary 零、 提案主旨與背景摘要

Background Pain Point: Imminent North India Capacity Vacuum

【背景痛點：迫在眉睫的北印度產能真空】

Auditing our major clients (existing clients like TATA, potential clients like Samsung, and clients under development), they strongly demand us to implement the "De-China / Made in India" strategy. However, Foxlink currently only has one base in South India (Tirupati) and faces a complete capacity vacuum in North India. This makes it impossible to serve key North Indian clients locally (e.g., Oppo, Samsung, VIVO, and major auto manufacturers), rendering business development extremely difficult. The previous strategy of "trading first, local manufacturing later" has proven ineffective.

盤點大客戶（現有客戶如TATA , 潛在客戶Samsung, 及開發中的客戶）強烈要求我們落實「去中化 / 印度製造」策略。然而，正崴目前在印度僅有南印（Tirupati）一處基地，在北印度面臨完全的產能真空，無法就近服務北印度關鍵客戶（如 Oppo, Samsung, VIVO, 各大車廠），業務開發非常困難, 之前測試的先貿易後本地製的策略沒有起到作用。

Resource Constraint: Traditional Factory Building is Too Slow

【資源困境：緩不濟急的傳統建廠模式】

If we adopt the traditional model of purchasing land and building a factory in North India, not only will it take 2-3 years, failing to meet urgent demands, but it will also severely crowd out the capital expenditure (CapEx) currently focused on other BUs by the Headquarters.

若採傳統模式在北印建廠，不僅耗時 2-3 年緩不濟急，且將嚴重排擠總部目前聚焦於其它BU的資本支出 (CapEx)。

Proposal Objective: 3-6 Months "Asset-Light Strategy" Blitz

【本案目的：3-6 個月閃電落地的「輕資產戰略」】

This proposal aims to present a breakthrough strategy to the HQ characterized by "Zero Capital Investment, Zero Equity Risk". We plan to utilize the massive existing facilities and equipment of local Tier-2 EMS providers in North India (e.g., Sahasra, VIVAAN), combined with Foxlink's exclusive "Core Material Black Box Protection" mechanism, transforming Foxlink into a "Supply Chain Control Tower".

本提案旨在向總部彙報一項「零資本投資、零股權風險」的破局戰略。我們計畫利用北印度在地 二線EMS（如 Sahasra、VIVAAN）現成的龐大廠房與設備，搭配正崴獨家的「核心物料黑盒防護」機制，讓正崴轉型為「供應鏈控制塔」。

Decision Request:【決策請求】 We beseech the HQ executives to review this "Certified Subcontractor Defensive Model" and approve this strategic framework as our primary weapon for North India business development. 懇請總部長官審閱此「特許代工防禦模式」，並以此戰略框架做為北印業務開發的武器。
1. Strategic Baseline: HQ's Absolute Bottom Lines & Core Targets 壹、 戰略定調：總部的絕對底線與核心目標

Facing the capacity gap (Oppo, Samsung, VIVO) and strict "China Plus One / Made in India" demands from global clients, this proposal establishes the following absolute bottom lines:

面對北印度產能缺口（Oppo, Samsung, VIVO）以及全球大客戶嚴格的「China Plus One / 印度製造」要求，本提案基於以下狀況設計，確立以下絕對底線：

Zero CapEx

Foxlink will NOT purchase any land, factory buildings, or heavy asset equipment in North India.

正崴不在北印度購買任何土地、廠房或重資產設備 (零資本支出)。

Foxlink will NOT establish a massive resident management and staffing structure here.

正崴不在此建立龐大的常駐管理與編制團隊 (零營運支出)。

Zero Equity Risk

NO Joint Ventures / NO Equity Investments.

不合資 / 不入股 (零股權風險)。

Core Objective: The Control Tower 核心目標： Transform Foxlink into a "Supply Chain Control Tower", utilizing the ready-made facilities and capital of multiple potential North Indian partners (e.g., Sahasra / VIVAAN, collectively referred to as "Local Factories") to fulfill our orders. Through a Multi-Vendor Strategy, ensure Foxlink holds absolute pricing and control power. 將正崴轉型為「供應鏈控制塔 (Control Tower)」，利用北印度多家潛在合作方（如：Sahasra / VIVAAN，以下統稱「本地廠」）的現成廠房與資金，來代工我們的北印客戶訂單。透過多供應商競局 (Multi-Vendor Strategy)，確保正崴掌握絕對的議價與控制權。
2. SWOT Analysis: Facing "Dominant Local Factories" 貳、 戰略環境評估 (SWOT)：面對「強勢本地廠」的現實

The subcontractors we seek (like Sahasra, already listed and supplying Hero; VIVAAN with TP-Link & Airtel track records) are not weak. Facing dominant and ambitious local giants, our SWOT assessment is as follows:

我們尋求的代工方（如 Sahasra 已經上市且切入 Hero 車廠；VIVAAN 則具備 TP-Link、Airtel 代工實績）皆非弱者。面對具備規模與野心的強勢地頭蛇，戰略優劣勢評估如下：

S: Strengths (Our Moat)S (Strengths - 我們的護城河)

  • Tier-1 Vendor Code: No matter how big local factories are, the Master Supply Agreements (MSA) and trust of US giants (Apple, Starlink, DELL, Tesla) remain firmly in Foxlink's hands. They cannot get this on their own.
  • 全球頂級客戶信任 (Vendor Code)： 即使本地廠規模再大，Apple、Starlink、DELL、Tesla 等美系大廠的 MSA (主供應商合約) 與信任，仍牢牢掌握在 Foxlink 手中。這是他們目前自己拿不到的。
  • Core Tech Barrier: Absolute advantage in high-frequency transmission and precision auto-grade connectors.
  • 核心零組件技術門檻： Foxlink 在高頻傳輸、精密車規連接器等技術上具有絕對優勢。
  • Zero Financial Risk: Adhering to Zero CapEx avoids massive sunk costs and depreciation pressure in India.
  • 零財務風險： 貫徹 Zero CapEx，不承擔印度建廠的龐大沉沒成本與折舊壓力。

W: Weaknesses (Our Achilles' Heel)W (Weaknesses - 我們的軟肋)

  • "Rootless" Status: No physical factory or personnel in North India. Against dominant local factories, our operational control is easily diluted by physical reality (they own the land, equipment, and labor).
  • 缺乏實體資產與「無根」狀態： 在北印度沒有自己的實體廠房與人員。面對強勢的本地廠，我們的營運控制力容易被「物理現實」（土地、設備、工人都是對方的）所削弱。
  • Lack of Management Penetration: We cannot micro-manage like our own factories; we must rely entirely on audits and contractual bindings.
  • 管理穿透力不足： 無法像經營自家工廠一樣微觀管理 (Micro-manage)，只能依賴稽核與合約約束。

O: Opportunities (Our Leverage)O (Opportunities - 我們的槓桿)

  • Blitz Scaling: Borrowing mature capacity compresses time-to-production from 2 years to 3-6 months, perfectly catching urgent "China Plus One" orders.
  • 閃電戰搶市佔： 借用強勢廠的現成成熟產能，能將建廠投產時間從 2 年壓縮至 3-6 個月，完美接住 China Plus One 的急單。
  • Bidding War: Since Sahasra & VIVAAN are both strong, Foxlink can play them against each other to drive down assembly quotes and force them to absorb RBA upgrade costs.
  • 多方競局 (Bidding War)： 由於 Sahasra 與 VIVAAN 皆具備足夠實力，Foxlink 可利用兩強相爭，壓低代工報價並逼迫其吸收 RBA 升級成本。
  • Brand Halo Incentive: Local factories desperately crave the track record of being a "Global Top-Tier Brand Certified Factory" to boost stock prices or future orders.
  • 品牌背書誘因： 本地廠極度渴望透過 Foxlink 取得「全球頂級品牌認證工廠」的實績，以利其自身股價或未來接單。

T: Threats (Our Fatal Risks)T (Threats - 我們的致命危機)

  • High Reverse Engineering Risk: Their engineering teams are mature. If not strictly blocked, they can crack the process/BOM and bypass Foxlink to contact end clients directly.
  • 高強度的逆向工程風險 (養虎為患)： 對方工程團隊成熟，若防堵不嚴密，極易被破解製程與 BOM 表，進而直接越過 Foxlink 接觸終端客戶。
  • Profit Sharing Conflicts: Listed/large factories won't settle for "meager assembly fees" long-term. They may threaten strikes, delays, or price hikes to force Foxlink to yield profit or technology.
  • 利潤分配衝突 (合作破裂)： 已上市或具規模的強勢廠，極可能不甘於長期只賺取「微薄的純代工費」，未來可能以罷工、拖延交期或威脅漲價來逼迫 Foxlink 讓利或釋放技術。
3. Core Operational Model: "Buy-Sell & Black Box" 參、 核心運作模式：「特許代工」與「核心物料黑盒化」

Based on the SWOT, to mitigate the Threats of strong subcontractors, we strictly position the local factory as a "Foxlink Exclusive Certified Subcontractor". The mechanism is as follows:

基於上述 SWOT 評估，為了防堵強勢代工廠的威脅 (Threats)，我們將本地廠嚴格定位為 「正崴獨家認證的特許代工廠 (Certified Subcontractor)」。具體運作機制如下：

A. Cracking the Strict DELL / RBA Audits: "Guided Compliance" over "Self-Building"

A. 破解 DELL / RBA 嚴苛稽核：以「輔導過關」取代「親自建廠」

  • Clients (like DELL) won't accept unverified local Indian factories. We don't pretend it's Foxlink's own factory; we declare it as a "dedicated subcontracting line under Foxlink's extreme strict control and guidance".
  • 客戶（如 DELL）不會接受未經驗證的印度本土廠。我們不假裝這是正崴的廠，而是向客戶表明這是「受正崴極度嚴格管控與輔導的專屬代工線」。
  • Cost Transfer: Foxlink only sends NPI and QA teams on short-term/consultant basis for guidance. To secure this international order, the local factory MUST fund themselves to upgrade security, improve working hour systems, and EHS conditions to meet Foxlink & client RBA standards. Foxlink is selling "the ticket and Know-how to enter the Tier-1 supply chain".
  • 成本轉嫁： 正崴僅派出 NPI 與 QA 團隊以「短期出差/顧問」形式前往指導。為了接下這筆國際大單，本地廠必須自行出資 升級廠區安保、改善工時系統與 EHS 條件，直到符合正崴與客戶的 RBA 標準。正崴賣的是「帶你打入 Tier-1 供應鏈的門票與 Know-how」。

B. Cracking the Order Poaching Crisis (Choke Point): BOM Control & Black Box Modules

B. 破解偷單危機 (Choke Point)：BOM 表控制與黑盒模組 (Sub-assembly)

  • To prevent local factories from learning the production and directly contacting the end client, Foxlink ABSOLUTELY WILL NOT release full Turnkey procurement rights.
  • 為了防止本地廠學會生產後直接找終端客戶接單，正崴絕對不釋放完整的 Turnkey 採購權。
  • The most core, high-margin, patent-protected parts (e.g., high-freq connectors, key chips, precision cables) are centrally manufactured by Foxlink Taiwan/China/Tirupati as "Black Box Sub-assemblies".
  • 產品中最核心、高毛利、具備正崴專利的部分（例如：高頻連接器、關鍵晶片、精密線材），統一由正崴台灣/中國/Tirupati廠製作成 「黑盒模組 (Black Box Sub-assembly)」。
  • Foxlink provides these Black Boxes via "Consignment" or "Buy-Sell" to the local factory. The local factory can only procure zero-tech plastics/screws locally. They only earn meager "assembly processing fees", never grasp core tech, and clients cannot bypass Foxlink to buy products missing core modules.
  • 正崴將黑盒模組以「客供料 (Consignment)」或「買賣 (Buy-Sell)」方式交給本地廠。本地廠在當地只能採購無技術含量的塑膠件、螺絲。他們只能賺取微薄的「組裝加工費」，永遠無法掌握核心技術，客戶也無法繞過正崴購買缺乏核心模組的產品。

PS. (SSSTC also imported equipment/materials to the FOXLINK India factory for processing and delivery to DELL, rather than building their own factory in India. The RBA costs were also borne by FOXLINK.)

PS. (SSSTC也是將設備/材料導入到FOXLINK印度廠加工後交DELL, 而不是自行來印度建廠, RBA費用也是 FOXLINK自行負責。)

4. Negotiation Strategy: Making Local Chairmen Agree (Trojan Horse) 肆、 談判策略：如何讓本地廠董事長點頭？ (特洛伊木馬與賽局戰略)

From the perspective of local factories, investing capital/effort only to earn assembly fees will cause immense initial resistance. Therefore, our outward negotiation must use "phased baits" coupled with "backup competition":

站在本地廠（如 Sahasra 董事長 Amrit 或 VIVAAN MD）的角度，他們出錢出力卻只能賺代工費，初期抗拒感必然極高。因此，我們在對外談判時，必須使用「階段性誘餌」搭配「備備胎競爭」來引導入局：

Bait 1: The Tier-1 Halo 誘餌一：全球 Tier-1 的光環 (The Tier-1 Halo) Local Indian EMS factories share a pain point: inability to enter top global supply chains. We clarify we bring stepping stones for Starlink, DELL, Top Auto OEMs. The "Foxlink Certified" label acts as priceless advertising, massively boosting their valuation. 印度本土 EMS 廠的共同痛點是「打不進全球頂級供應鏈」。我們明確告知，正崴帶來的不是普通的單，而是 Starlink、DELL、一線車廠的敲門磚。只要產線掛上「正崴認證生產」，該集團在印度的估值與接單能力將產生質的飛躍。這對他們來說是無價的廣告。
Bait 2: The Illusion of Localization 誘餌二：未來的「在地化大餅」 (The Illusion of Localization) We mandate Black Box for Phase 1 to "ensure FAI pass". But we verbally promise (contingency): "If Phase 1 succeeds, Phase 2 involves Phased Licensing for local procurement/production." Giving them future growth imagination to force early compromise. 初期以「確保品質通過客戶首件檢查 (FAI)」為由，強制實施核心料件黑盒化。但我們在口頭給予承諾：「只要 Phase 1 合作順利、良率達標，Phase 2 我們會逐步授權 (Phased Licensing) 更多零件讓本地廠在地採購或生產。」 給予未來成長想像空間，促使其初期妥協。
Moat Excuse: Tariffs & Yield Packing 護城河藉口：利用「關稅」與「良率」包裝技術封鎖 Our official PR for blocking direct core procurement: "India's import tariffs on high-freq components are huge, and testers are expensive. Foxlink 'kindly' sends pre-made modules to save your CapEx and guarantee yield. We 'loan' the tester." (In reality, it's locked to HQ servers). 針對為何不讓他們直接採購核心零件，官方說辭是：「印度針對某些高頻電子元件進口關稅極高，且測試機台極其昂貴。為了幫本地廠節省初期成本並確保良率，正崴『好心』將模組做好送過來，並將專用設備『借放』廠內（實則連線總部伺服器，鎖死參數）。」
Negotiation Leverage: The Bidding War 談判槓桿：創造「多方競局 (Bidding War)」 We won't negotiate exclusively with Sahasra. We'll intentionally let them know we are evaluating VIVAAN (and vice versa). Exploiting their hunger for Tier-1 orders forces them to make maximum concessions on Margins and RBA investments. 我們不會只與 Sahasra 單線談判。我們會刻意讓 Sahasra 知道我們同時在評估 VIVAAN（反之亦然）。利用他們渴望搶下 Foxlink / Tier-1 訂單的心態，迫使他們在代工費 (Margin) 與 RBA 投資上做出最大讓步。

6. Consultant Pre-Audit: Execution Blind Spots & Defense Plans 陸、 顧問預審 (Consultant Pre-Audit)：落地實戰盲區與防禦備案

To prevent the strategy from backfiring due to India's complex business/tax environment, we have pre-prepared defensive solutions for potential queries from HQ Legal/Finance:

為確保戰略落地時不被印度本地複雜的商業與稅務環境反噬，針對總部法務/財務可能提出的質疑，我們已預先備妥以下防禦性解決方案：

Q1
Blind Spot 1: Who bears responsibility if local factory invests but fails DELL RBA Audit? 盲區一：若本地廠投入資金卻未通過客戶 RBA 稽核，誰扛責？

Challenge: Indian factories generally lack awareness of labor hours and security. If DELL fails them causing order loss, they will demand Foxlink compensate their hardware investments.

挑戰： 印度工廠普遍對工時、廠區安保認知薄弱，若 DELL 稽核死當導致訂單流失，本地廠必定回頭向正崴索賠其初期硬體投資。

Defense Plan [Tollgate Milestone]: 防禦備案【Tollgate 里程碑機制】： Insert a hard "Pre-audit Tollgate" clause. Foxlink absolutely will NOT bind the client's Vendor Code to that line until Foxlink NPI/QA formally signs off. The contract will specify RBA passing as a "Condition Precedent" for formal orders, exempting Foxlink from joint liability. 在合約中強硬加入「前置稽核條款 (Pre-audit Tollgate)」。在 Foxlink NPI/QA 團隊未正式簽字放行前，正崴絕對不將客戶的 Vendor Code 綁定該產線。合約明訂 RBA 過關為正式下單之「先決條件 (Condition Precedent)」，免除正崴的連帶賠償責任。
Q2
Blind Spot 2: Tax Traps & Working Capital Conflicts from "Black Box Buy-Sell" 盲區二：「黑盒買賣」引發的稅務陷阱與 Working Capital 衝突

Challenge: Forcing local factories to "buy" our expensive Black Boxes (Buy-Sell) will trigger cross-state IGST or import tariffs, and severely freeze their working capital, causing strong resistance.

挑戰： 若要求本地廠「買下」正崴高單價的黑盒模組（Buy-Sell Model），將面臨印度跨邦 IGST 稅務或進口關稅，且會嚴重卡死本地廠的營運資金，導致對方強烈抗拒。

Defense Plan [Pure Consignment & Job Work Invoice]: 防禦備案【純客供與加工發票】： Empower negotiation team with flexibility. If they refuse Buy-Sell, instantly revert to "Pure Consignment". Foxlink retains ownership of expensive modules, merely "parking" materials on the local line. They only issue a "Job Work" invoice to us. This solves their cash flow pressure and legally cements their status as a mere "processing party". 授權談判團隊保有彈性。若對方拒絕買賣模式，可立即退回「純客供料 (Consignment)」模式。正崴保有高價模組的所有權，僅將料件「寄放」於本地產線，本地廠僅開立「Job Work（加工服務）」發票給我們。此舉不僅解決對方的資金壓力，在法律上更進一步坐實了他們「只是純加工方」的底層地位。
Q3
Blind Spot 3: Dominant Factories Will Absolutely Demand "Capacity Guarantee (MOQ)" 盲區三：強勢廠絕對會反向要求「產能保證 (MOQ)」

Challenge: To de-risk their investment, local factories will demand: "Foxlink must commit to N thousand units/month, otherwise compensate SMT idle fees."

挑戰： 本地廠為了降低投資風險，合約中必會要求：「Foxlink 需承諾每月至少 N 萬套的訂單，否則需賠償 SMT 產線閒置費」。

Defense Plan [Back-to-Back Clause]: 防禦備案【Back-to-Back 背靠背條款】： Foxlink's bottom line is "absolutely NO bearing idle costs for Indian factories". The contract will adopt a strict back-to-back mechanism: Any MOQ commitment is strictly pre-conditioned on "the formal PO from end clients (Starlink/DELL) being issued". We only act as a "Pass-through", perfectly transferring risk to actual end-market demand. 正崴的底線是「絕對不扛印度廠的閒置成本」。合約將採用嚴格的背靠背機制：任何對本地廠的 MOQ 承諾，其生效前提皆為「終端客戶 (如 Starlink/DELL) 的正式 PO 已下達」。我們只負責「Pass-through (轉發)」，將風險完美轉嫁給終端市場的實際需求。
