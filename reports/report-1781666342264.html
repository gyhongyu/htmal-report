Dual-Track Market Model

雙軌制通路體系

  • B2B Utility Megaprojects (70%): Negotiates directly with state-run and top-tier private power developers (Adani Green, SECI, Tata Power) for solar module supply and turnkey EPC grid setup.
  • B2B 大型公用事業電站 (約 70%)： 直接對接國營與大型民營開發商（如 Adani Green, SECI, Tata Power），提供模組及一站式變電併網 EPC 服務。
  • B2C Retail & Distributed Rooftops (30%): Branded under "Waaree Prime+". Powered by 369+ franchisee outlets in high-radiation states (Rajasthan, Gujarat, Maharashtra).
  • B2C 零售與分布式屋頂 (約 30%)： 主打「Waaree Prime+」品牌。截至 2026 年初，在全印設有 369+ 個特許加盟商網點，深度覆蓋西部與南部光伏資源豐富的邦。
  • Dealer Digital Ecosystem: Developed proprietary B2B dispatch portal "Waaree Connect" for dealers to track live inventory, secure credit lines, and register warranty codes in one click. Does not rely on Amazon/Flipkart.
  • 經銷商數位生態系： 建立自有的數位 B2B 採購系統 "Waaree Connect"，供全國加盟商與 EPC 承包商即時進行庫存查詢、在線下單與物流追蹤。零售端裝機則透過「Waaree Prime+」App 完成。

Logistics & Warehousing Logic

供應鏈物流配送邏輯

  • Central Dispatch Nodes: Dispatches directly from Chikhli and Tumb central assembly warehouses in Gujarat. Cargo is routed to 5 strategic Regional Distribution Centers (Regional DCs).
  • 中央發貨節點： 產品統一由吉吉拉特邦的 Chikhli 與 Tumb 中央倉發出，輻射至印度的五大區域分撥中心 (Regional DCs)。
  • JIT Demand for Vendors: Operates on an aggressive "Just-In-Time" (JIT) schedule due to massive daily manufacturing volume. Requires key component vendors to maintain a 2-4 week safety stock buffer physically located in India.
  • 供應商 JIT 機制： 由於日均出貨量巨大，Waaree 採取「Just-In-Time (JIT)」的嚴格倉儲配送機制，要求供應商在印度的常備庫存需保持 2-4 週的安全水平。
  • Authorized Service Network: Supported by 500+ authorized retail service and technical maintenance points for instant troubleshooting.
  • 售後服務網絡： 擁有 500+ 個零售服務與技術維修合作網點，提供即時售後檢測。
