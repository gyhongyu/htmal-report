Foxlink Strategic Entry Analysis (Opportunities)

正崴精密 (Foxlink) 戰略切入契機與機遇

1. BMS & PCBA Manufacturing 1. BMS 與高階電子組裝代工

Amara Raja's electronics arm (AREL) lacks highly sophisticated SMT processing required for advanced automotive-grade and high-safety storage BMS. Foxlink's massive global PCBA procurement and high-precision SMT lines present an ideal manufacturing partner option.

Amara Raja 旗下電子廠 (AREL) 雖然具備 SMT 能力，但在汽車級和高安全儲能級的高階 BMS 製造經驗與良率仍有侷限。正崴領先的 PCBA 生產管理與晶片採購優勢是最佳合作切入點。

2. High-Voltage Connectors & Cables 2. 高壓連接器與重載動力線束

Lithium battery pack interiors, EV fast chargers, and grid-scale storage units require severe-vibration resilient, high-current connectors and high-voltage wiring harnesses. This sits perfectly within Foxlink's core capability matrix.

鋰電池包 (Battery Pack) 內部高壓串聯以及外部充電樁需要大量的重載、抗震、高氣密耐溫連接器及高壓動力線束，這正是正崴精密的核心看家本領與既有優勢。

3. PLI Scheme & Local Sourcing Push 3. 響應 PLI 與在地製造防線

The Indian government levies steep import duties on lithium-ion cells and EV components while offering lucrative PLI subsidies. Amara Raja urgently needs premium, localized Tier 1 suppliers within India to satisfy local value addition targets and counter Exide's advances.

印度 government 對鋰電池及電動車零部件實施高昂關稅，並給予 PLI 補貼。Amara Raja 強烈需要能在印度本地製造、且符合本土增值率規定的高品質供應商，以抵禦外資競爭並取得補貼優勢。
