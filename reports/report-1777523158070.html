Pure B2B & SI Model

  • Direct OEM/ODM: No general consumer market. Direct engagements with Industrial, Medical or Gov entities (e.g., AAI).
  • 代工與直銷： 不經營大眾市場。直接對接工業客戶、醫療設備廠或政府機關。
  • Digital Presence: Corporate showcase only. RFP-driven pipeline, no B2C e-commerce.
  • 數位平台： 官網純粹展示解決方案。需透過聯繫進入 RFP 階段，無電商。

Supply Chain Logic

供應鏈物流邏輯

  • Project-Based: Highly erratic schedules. Build/Configure to Order (BTO/CTO).
  • 專案導向採購： 承接 BTO/CTO 訂單，交貨排程不固定且規格殊異。
  • Agility Required: Vendors must possess extreme flexibility to handle sudden, specialized batches.
  • 極致彈性： 供應商必須具備高度彈性，能配合其少量多樣的特殊交期。
