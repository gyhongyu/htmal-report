BOM Analysis (Teardown)
主要零組件技術拆解
  • Cell & Energy: In-house 4680 spec Cylindrical Lithium Cell (Bharat Cell - LFP & High-Nickel NMC chemistry).
  • 電芯能源: 自研 4680 圓柱鋰電芯 (Bharat Cell - LFP / 高鎳 NMC 體系) 與高強度防爆模組。
  • BMS System: Multi-layer controller PCBA modules, precise current sensors, MOSFET safety switches.
  • 電池管理 (BMS): 多層主控 PCB 板、精密檢測傳感器與安全保護開關。
  • Drive & MCU: Mid-mounted IPM Motors, Hub Motors (S1 Air/X series) & high-density controllers.
  • 馬達控制器: 中置內置永磁同步馬達 (IPM)、輪轂馬達與驅動器 (MCU)。
  • VCU & HMI: Vehicle Control Unit with integrated 4G LTE/GPS, 7-inch TFT smart display running MoveOS.
  • 控制與人機 (VCU/HMI): 整合 4G LTE/GPS 通訊模組與 7 吋 MoveOS 觸控面板。

5. Sales & Channel Strategy

5. 銷售與通路佈局

Direct to Consumer (D2C) Model

直營 D2C 混合模式 (Pure D2C Hybrid)

  • By-passed Dealers: Bypassed traditional multi-layered dealership channels. Unified pan-India pricing, keeping profits entirely with HQ.
  • 不設傳統經銷商： 捨棄印度傳統多級經銷商，採取線上與直營結合，全國統一售價，利潤完全收回總部。
  • Experience Centres: Over 800+ company-owned physical centers for demo rides, MoveOS software walk-through, and direct ordering.
  • 體驗中心佈局： 全印開設 800+ 家直營體驗店 (Experience Centres)，提供試乘、車機導覽與客戶現場下單。
  • App Centricity: 90% of bookings, parts sourcing, insurance and finance handled directly through the official "Ola Electric App".
  • App 極致閉環： 90% 的新車、配件訂購與金融保險服務均於「Ola Electric App」直接成交。

Logistics Logic

供應鏈物流邏輯

  • Central Dispatch Hub: Component parts from Chennai Port or local Tier-1s flow to Krishnagiri; finished EVs dispatch directly to state-level Delivery Hubs or customer homes.
  • 集中發貨中心： 零部件自欽奈港或本地廠商送往 Krishnagiri；整車出廠後直調各邦「Delivery Hub (交付中心)」或送貨上門。
  • VMI / JIT Expectation: Suppliers must match higher frequency dispatch. Needs excellent logistics and "Zero Inventory" storage setup near plant.
  • 高頻 JIT 需求： 供應商需具備高彈性供貨與「零庫存 (VMI/JIT)」管理能力，在工廠周邊建置對接點。
  • Hyperservice Support: Over 600+ physical service setups matched with "Mobile Service Vans" to tackle remote breakdowns, alongside their own Hypercharger network.
  • 售後與充電網： 設有 600+ 直營服務網點，配備「Mobile Service Van」移動維修車；全國廣佈專屬 Hypercharger 快速充電站。
