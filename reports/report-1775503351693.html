2. Discussion Record 貳、 會議討論內容

Item 1: Cross-Border Operational Challenges & Regional Strategy

議題一：跨國營運挑戰與區域佈局戰略分析

Geopolitical restrictions continue to create "soft barriers" in supply chain management. Indian business visas for Chinese staff face high rejection rates and complex documentation requirements (e.g., proving multiple supplier interactions), forcing reliance on remote online collaboration for technical debugging.

當前地緣政治引發的行政限制干擾了供應鏈管理中的人才與技術流動。印度員工申請中國商務簽證面臨極高拒簽率與繁瑣文件需求，導致技術交流受阻，僅能依賴線上協作，對現場調試管理造成極大壓力。

  • Vietnam Expansion: VIVO noted Foxlink's expansion in Da Nang, Vietnam. Compared to the highly saturated and competitive Hanoi cluster, Da Nang offers strategic developmental potential despite being further from traditional component hubs.
  • 越南佈局差異化： VIVO 關注到 Foxlink 在越南峴港 (Da Nang) 的佈局，認為其相較於供應商飽和的北越河內，具備相對低競爭的勞動力戰略開發潛力。

Implication: As VIVO's core technical and purchasing decisions remain centralized in China, Foxlink must strengthen internal coordination between its China and India teams to bridge the cross-regional management gap and ensure Indian factory needs reach decision-makers.

戰略意涵： 由於 VIVO 的核心技術與採購決策權仍集中於中國總部，Foxlink 需在內部強化中、印兩地團隊的協作，確保印度端的需求能同步傳達至決策中樞。

Item 2: VIVO India Procurement Architecture & Technical Reality

議題二：VIVO 印度採購架構與技術深度解析

VIVO processes an astonishing 2 million components daily, necessitating a highly specialized division of labor.

VIVO 每日處理高達 200 萬個零件，為支撐此規模，其採購邏輯採高度職能化分工。

  • Organizational Split: Akash leads a 30-person "Corporate Procurement" team focused on raw materials and commercial negotiations, while CAPEX equipment and technical maintenance are handled by factory personnel.
  • 職能分工： 區分「企業採購」與「工廠技術」。Akash 領導的 30 人團隊專注原材料商務談判，廠端人員則負責 CAPEX 設備與技術。
  • Localization Limits: Despite increased local capabilities (like SMT), core technology components (resistors, SMT materials, LCM sensor boards) still heavily rely on imports from China.
  • 在地化極限： 儘管印度具備 SMT 能力，但核心技術件（電阻、SMT 材料、LCM 感測電路板）仍高度依賴中國進口體系。

With such massive daily volume, even minor delays (e.g., driver miscommunication) or accounting errors compound into massive costs. Suppliers must demonstrate extreme agility and digital tracking capabilities.

在巨量運作下，微小的物流延遲或財務核算錯誤皆會演變成巨大管理成本。供應商必須具備極強的即時應變與數位化追蹤能力。

Item 3: Supply Chain Reality & Vertical Integration Strategy

議題三：產品供應鏈現狀與垂直整合策略評估

Localization is not absolute; it is a complex calculation of cost, tariffs, and logistics volume.

電子產品的在地化並非盲目追求，而是成本預算、關稅變動與物流體積的綜合博弈。

  • "Reverse Localization" of FPCs: Due to a recent tariff reduction and the viability of rapid air-freight, FPC supply has strategically reverted to imports from China, where economies of scale outweigh local production benefits.
  • FPC「逆向在地化」： 因關稅調降及具備空運物流優勢，中國進口的規模經濟高於本地生產成本，使 FPC 策略性轉向依賴進口。
  • 100% Local Plastics: Conversely, bulky plastic components require local production for immediate quality control (feedback on black spots, transparency), though mold designs remain tightly controlled by China.
  • 塑膠件 100% 在地化： 塑膠件因體積大且需即時解決品質變數（黑點、透明度等），必須本地生產（但模具設計仍由中方管控）。

Strategic Moat (Cables & Connectors): Foxlink proposed introducing high-precision cable extrusion processes to India. Currently, the Indian market is dominated by thick automotive cables. This vertical integration builds a competitive barrier and effectively counters the de-localization risks seen with FPCs.

戰略護城河（傳輸線與連接器）： Foxlink 提議引進高精密線材擠出 (Extrusion) 製程。填補了目前印度以厚重汽車級電纜為主的市場缺口。此垂直整合不僅提升競爭門檻，更能有效反制類 FPC 產品的去在地化風險。

Item 4: Market Competition & Management Philosophy

議題四：市場競爭格局與技術趨勢洞察

VIVO is actively pushing for premium brand positioning. Their X series foldables (priced at 1.5 Lakh) are engineered to rival or surpass Samsung in durability and form factor. Akash used a "Toyota vs. Lexus" analogy for BBK group brands to explain their premium perception strategy.

在紅海競爭中，VIVO 透過品牌區隔建立溢價空間。會中展示的高端 X 系列折疊機（定價 15 萬盧比），在硬體上已評估為超越三星。Akash 以「豐田與凌志」類比 BBK 集團內的品牌感知管理。

AI vs. Human Trust: While acknowledging that AI tools (like DeepSeek) drastically improve efficiency, Akash stressed that business fundamentally relies on human relationships ("never eat and drink alone"). For the post-90s management tier, relationship-building and trust at the negotiation table remain irreplaceable by AI.

AI 與人際互信的平衡： 儘管 DeepSeek 等 AI 工具大幅提升了效率，但 Akash 引用其上級哲學「絕不單獨進食與飲酒」，強調採購談判的核心仍是餐桌上的溝通與互信。對於「90 後」管理者而言，AI 只是工具，真正的商業決策依賴人際信任積累。
