2. Strategic Objectives 貳、 參訪目的與戰略效益

Integrating this expo into the April itinerary allows us to gain a macro view of the Indian electronics market efficiently. The visit will precisely focus on three strategic areas:

將此展會納入 4 月份行程，旨在以最高效率獲取印度電子製造市場的全局視野。本次參訪將精準聚焦以下三大戰略重點，協助總部評估佈局：

1. Evaluate "Make in India" Supply Chain Maturity 1. 實地評估「在地製造 (Make in India)」之必要性與供應鏈現況

Import Substitution Assessment: By inspecting local EMS players, we will observe which components are completely localized and which still rely on imports. This real-world data will directly inform HQ's decision on "which product lines must be produced in India vs. supported from China."

進口替代空間評估： 透過視察本土 EMS 廠與供應商，觀察目前哪些零組件已實現完全在地採購，哪些仍高度依賴海外進口。這將為決策 「哪些產品線必須在印度生產，哪些可繼續由中國廠區支援」 提供最直接的數據。

2. Explore Cross-BU Expansion (Beyond Cable/SMT) 2. 探索 Cable 與 SMT 以外的 BU 產品推廣機會

The expo gathers end-manufacturers across Consumer, Telecom (e.g., VIVO), and Automotive electronics (e.g., Uno Minda). We will use this to:

展會匯聚了消費性電子、通訊設備與車用電子（如 VIVO, Uno Minda）的終端製造商。我們將藉此：

  • Identify Hidden Demand: Gauge urgent local needs for other high-end components (precision connectors, power modules, mechanical parts).
  • 發掘潛在需求： 透過買家詢價焦點，了解當地對高階零組件（如：精密連接器、電源模組、機構件）的迫切需求。
  • Competitor Benchmarking: Monitor key competitors' flagship products and localization footprint in India.
  • 競品動態對標： 觀察主要競爭對手在印度的主推產品與在地化佈局，評估引進 Foxlink 其他 BU 資源的可行性。

3. High-Efficiency Agile Networking 3. 高效商務拓展與機動性客戶對接

One-Stop Target Gathering: Procurement heads from major EMS companies (both existing and potential clients) will be present. We will maintain an agile schedule to facilitate impromptu executive meetings on-site, creating additional business breakthroughs outside our pre-planned itinerary.

潛在客戶一站式網羅： 名單上的既有與潛在客戶（大廠採購主管）幾乎都會出席。我們將保持行程機動性，若接觸到關鍵決策者，可直接在現場進行 高效會談，為出差創造額外的商務突破口。

Conclusion: A single-day inspection of Electronica India fills the "macro-market gap" that individual client visits cannot provide, directly assisting executives in validating strategic assumptions for Foxlink's Indian expansion.

結論： 安排單日視察 Electronica India，不僅能補足單一客戶拜訪無法看清的「整體市場宏觀格局」，更能直接協助高層驗證對印度產能配置與產品擴充的戰略假設。
