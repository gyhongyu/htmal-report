Distributed Setup & Customer Proximity Log

分散式設廠與客戶就近配送邏輯

  • Customer Proximity (50-km Radius): AC structures, heavy metal shells, and blowers carry massive shipping overheads. Amber installs its assembly units within a 50km radius of the client's manufacturing hubs (e.g. Pune for LG, Sriperumbudur for South makers). 就近設廠 (50公里圈): 由於金屬殼體與熱交換器體積大、物流費用高，Amber 採取「客戶工廠 50 公里內建廠」策略（如在 Pune 服務 LG，在南部服務大金）。
  • Foxlink Collaborative Logic:
    To capture Amber's BOM share, Foxlink needs local JIT dispatch support near Greater Noida, Pune, or the new Hosur bare PCB cluster, or provide dedicated bonded warehouses for smooth daily drop-offs. 正崴若與其深度合作，必須在 Greater Noida / Jewar、Hosur 園區或 Pune，具備「當地供貨」或「保稅倉快速交貨 (JIT)」之能力。
