5. Sales & Channel Strategy

5. 銷售與通路佈局

Dual Track Model

雙軌模式 (Dual Track)

  • OEM Direct (B2B): The lion's share of revenue. Deep "Design-in" partnerships right from the vehicle development phase.
  • OEM 直供 (B2B)： 佔絕大營收比例，參與車廠前期研發與規格制定 (Design-in)。
  • Aftermarket (B2B2C): Over 300+ 'DENSO Service Networks (DIAS)' across India. Strong in AC comps, wipers, spark plugs.
  • 售後市場 (Aftermarket)： 全印 300+ 授權維修網 (DIAS)，專攻冷氣壓縮機、雨刷及火星塞。
  • Digital E-Comm: No direct D2C Amazon store; relies on Boodmo & regional auto part distributors.
  • 數位電商： 無官方 Amazon 旗艦店，依賴經銷商於 Boodmo 等汽配專屬平台上架。

Supply Chain Logistics Logic

供應鏈物流邏輯

  • OEM Proximity: Physical factories bonded tightly with Maruti Suzuki (Manesar), Honda (Tapukara), and Toyota (Bidadi).
  • 大客戶綁定： 廠區緊鄰 Maruti Suzuki, Honda 與 Toyota，實現廠對廠物理綁定。
  • Vendor Mandate: Tier 2 suppliers MUST implement VMI (Vendor Managed Inventory) or establish a dispatch warehouse within 50km of Denso facilities.
  • 二階供應商要求： 強制要求 Tier 2 必須建置 VMI 系統，或在 Denso 廠區 50 公里內設立緩衝發貨倉。
