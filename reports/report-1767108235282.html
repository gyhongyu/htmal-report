2. Discussion Record 貳、 會議討論內容

Item 1: Competitive Landscape & Operational Presence

議題一：競爭態勢與在地營運現況

ADATA has established a mature physical factory in Noida Sector 138 with fully independent SMT production lines. They currently operate both brand sales and OEM/ODM services, rendering them a direct competitor to Foxlink's SMT assembly business in India. Their independence from external contract manufacturers gives them a significant agility advantage in the local market.

ADATA 在 Noida Sector 138 已建立成熟的實體工廠，具備完全自主的 SMT 生產線。目前除了自有品牌銷售外，亦積極承接 OEM/ODM 訂單，這使得 ADATA 在製造端與正崴印度廠的 SMT 代工業務屬於直接競爭對手。其不依賴外部代工的特性，賦予其在當地市場極高的運作靈活性。

Item 2: Strategic Pivot - Beyond Traditional OEM

議題二：戰略轉型 - 跳脫傳統 OEM 代工思維

Sonic Hsieh suggested that Foxlink, as a "Mega-Group," should avoid competing in the "Red Ocean" of low-margin SMT storage assembly. Instead, he recommended a "Leapfrog Deployment" strategy by integrating group resources:

Sonic Hsieh 先生建議，正崴身為「特大集團」，不應在低毛利的 SMT 存儲代工紅海中空轉。相反，應採取「超前佈署」戰略，整合集團資源：

  • Synergy: Combine **Ubilink (AI Computing)**, **Shinfox (Green Energy)**, and **SYNC ROBOTIC (Automation)** to offer systemic solutions.
  • 綜效整合： 結合 Ubilink (AI 算力)、Shinfox (綠能) 及 SYNC ROBOTIC (自動化)，提供系統性解決方案。
  • Differentiation: Leverage Foxlink's patents in high-speed connectors and power management to develop niche storage for AI servers or rugged industrial use.
  • 差異化： 利用正崴在高速連接器與電源管理的專利，開發針對 AI 伺服器或強固型工業用的特殊存儲產品。

Item 3: Potential "Mega-Project" Collaboration

議題三：潛在「大型專案」合作可行性

Despite the manufacturing overlap, Hsieh acknowledged potential for "Joint-Force" collaboration for large-scale Indian government tenders or hyperscale data centers. The logic is a complementary stack: ADATA provides core IP and DRAM/SSD components, while Foxlink provides system chassis, power infrastructure, and localized integration.

儘管製造端存在重疊，Hsieh 先生認可雙方在印度政府大型標案或超大規模資料中心專案中「強強聯手」的可能性。合作邏輯在於優勢互補：由 ADATA 提供核心技術 IP 與顆粒組件，正崴則提供系統機殼、電源基礎設施及在地整合組裝。
