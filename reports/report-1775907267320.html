Go-To-Market Tactics

市場拓展戰術 (GTM)

  • Direct Outreach: Proactively reach out to potential clients with tailored value propositions.
  • 主動出擊 (Direct Outreach)： 帶著量身定制的價值主張，主動接觸潛在客戶。
  • Relationship Leverage: Utilize existing client relationships to gain insights and referrals.
  • 關係槓桿 (Relationship Leverage)： 善用現有客戶關係網，獲取內部情報與引薦。
  • Escalation Handling: Always talk & share with your Manager. Don't fear client escalation if the internal team knows the full story.
  • 危機升級處理： 遇到專案停滯務必求助。只要內部資訊透明，無需畏懼客戶端的 Escalation，甚至應主動利用 Escalation 來推動專案進度。
