Logistics Logic

供應鏈物流邏輯

  • Cluster Manufacturing: Plants are strategically located adjacent to client OEM parks (e.g., inside or near Maruti's compound).
  • 聚落製造 (Cluster Mfg)： 工廠緊貼客戶園區佈局 (例如在 Maruti Suzuki 園區旁設廠)。
  • VMI (Vendor Managed Inventory): High expectation for vendors to maintain local buffer stocks to support JIT lines.
  • VMI 管理： 供應商需具備 VMI (供應商管理庫存) 能力，以支援其 JIT 生產線。
