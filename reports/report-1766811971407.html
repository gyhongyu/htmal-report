2. Discussion Record 貳、 會議討論內容

Executive Summary & Context

專案背景與執行摘要

The project has been stagnant for 9 months since its initiation in March. The supplier headquarters has already invested capital in a dedicated production line and is inquiring about the delay. The primary objective of this meeting was to identify the root causes of this stagnation and realign on contract procedures and cost structures to unlock the project.

本專案自今年三月啟動以來，已歷時九個月仍未進入實質量產階段。由於供應商總部已投入資本建設專用產線，目前面臨極大的投資回報壓力。本次會議的核心目的，即是找出導致專案停滯的「根本原因」，並釐清雙方對於合約流程與供應鏈責任的重大認知落差，以期重啟專案。

Item 1: Contract Finalization & Legal Team Requirements

議題一：合約簽署瓶頸與溝通語言規範

Context & Issue 現況與問題

The supplier has attempted to negotiate specific contract terms but has faced significant delays. It was clarified that Vivo's legal team operates independently from procurement and consists entirely of Indian legal professionals.

專案延宕的主因被鎖定在「合約簽署」環節。供應商過去試圖針對合約條款進行協商，但發現回覆極度緩慢。經確認，Vivo 的法務審查團隊由印度本地律師組成，且獨立於採購部門運作。

Key Operational Constraints 關鍵作業限制
  • Language Barrier: Any communication or contract revision containing Chinese text is unreadable to the Indian legal team, causing the file to be ignored or rejected immediately.
  • 語言隔閡： 過去往來的郵件或合約修訂若夾雜中文，印度法務無法閱讀，導致案件直接被擱置或退回。
  • Review Cycle: Any deviation from Vivo's standard template triggers a mandatory review process that takes a minimum of 3 months.
  • 審查週期： 任何對標準合約的修改，都必須經過至少 3 個月 的強制審查週期，這是導致專案卡關九個月的結構性原因。
Strategic Decision 決策與行動

To bypass this bottleneck, strict protocol is now established: All future communication must be in English only. Furthermore, the supplier will evaluate accepting the standard contract terms to avoid the 3-month delay.

為繞過此障礙，雙方達成戰術共識：即刻起，所有與 Vivo 往來的郵件、合約修訂與附件，必須全數使用英文。嚴禁出現中文說明。同時，供應商將重新評估是否直接接受標準條款，以換取專案即刻啟動。

Item 2: Supply Chain Model & Financial Impact

議題二：供應鏈模型校準與財務損益修正

Context & Misconception 認知落差與背景

The supplier previously operated under the assumption that they were required to hold 30 days of safety stock (carrying heavy holding costs), which inflated their internal financial forecasts and reduced quotation competitiveness.

在成本結構上，供應商一直存在嚴重誤解，誤以為必須承擔高額的庫存持有成本 (30天安全庫存)，這導致內部的財務預測偏高，影響了報價競爭力與合作意願。

Policy Correction 政策導正
  • Old Belief: 30 Days Stock (Split holding).
  • 舊認知 (錯誤)： 供應商需備 30 天安全庫存。
  • Official Policy: 10 Days Total Stock, held 100% by Vivo.
  • 新事實 (正確)： Vivo 政策為「庫存即成本」。總安全庫存僅需 10 天，且完全由 Vivo 自行持有。
Financial Implication 戰略意義

This clarification effectively removes the inventory holding cost from the supplier's P&L for this project. Finance must re-calculate the project profitability based on this "Zero Holding Cost" model.

此澄清對 Foxlink 極為有利。這意味著我們不需要在當地額外租賃倉庫或積壓資金。財務部門需立即根據此「零庫存持有成本」的新模型，重新計算專案損益 (P&L)，這將大幅提升本案的淨利率預期。
