2. Discussion Record 貳、 會議討論內容

Item 1: Foxlink’s Heritage & NCR Strategic Expansion

議題一：正崴集團背景優勢與 NCR 地區戰略佈局

Context: Michael Chen positioned Foxlink within the global manufacturing hierarchy, emphasizing the group's deep synergy with Foxconn while highlighting Foxlink's specialized dominance in Apple peripherals (cables, connectors, AirPods).

背景說明： Michael Chen 向對方說明正崴在全球製造體系的定位，強調與鴻海集團的淵源，並突出正崴在 Apple 供應鏈（連接線、接頭、AirPods 等周邊）的專業主導地位。

Implication: Establishing our identity as an "Apple-grade" manufacturer allows us to set a high technical benchmark, differentiating Foxlink from current local OEMs and validating the Gurgaon facility's premium capability.

戰略意義： 確立我方為「Apple 等級」製造商的身分，有助於建立技術門檻，使正崴與目前印度的在地 OEM 區隔開來，並強化古爾岡新廠的高階生產實力。

Item 2: Portronics’ Operational Gaps & Localized Demand

議題二：Portronics 營運缺口與在地化品質需求

Fact: Portronics currently manages 3 to 4 OEM vendors for their extensive product range. Mr. Jha expressed significant interest in EMS providers who can deliver localized manufacturing with international quality standards.

事實記錄： Portronics 目前針對其多元產品線由 3 至 4 家 OEM 供應商協助生產。Jha 先生表示，對於能在印度在地化並提供國際品質標準的 EMS 服務商展現高度興趣。

Implication: There is a clear strategic "opening" for Foxlink to capture market share from existing vendors by offering localized production that reduces import hurdles while maintaining premium quality control.

戰略意義： 正崴具備切入 Portronics 供應鏈的空隙，透過提供能降低進口障礙且具備頂尖品管的在地化生產，未來具備取代現有供應商或承接新產品線的優勢。

Item 3: Precision Demonstration - The Smart Ring Case Study

議題三：精密技術展示 - 智慧戒指案例研究

Detail: To demonstrate manufacturing finesse beyond standard peripherals, Michael presented a "Smart Ring" produced for a US client. This served as tangible evidence of Foxlink’s capability to handle compact, highly integrated consumer electronics.

詳細內容： 為展示超越標準周邊產品的精密製造工藝，Michael 展示了為美國客戶生產的「智慧戒指」。這成為正崴有能力處理體積細小、高集成度消費性電子的實物證明。

Implication: This visual and physical proof of concept significantly boosted Portronics' confidence in Foxlink’s technical depth, moving the conversation from general capacity to specific high-end project potential.

戰略意義： 此實物展示顯著提升了 Portronics 對正崴技術深度的信心，將討論層次從一般的產能供應提升至高階項目的合作潛力。
