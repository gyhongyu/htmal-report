5. Sales & Channel Strategy

5. 銷售與通路佈局

Channel Strategy

通路策略概況

  • Direct B2B/B2G: Zero retail/e-commerce presence. Targets Indian Army, Paramilitary, and internal Reliance networks (Jio Telecom towers, Refinery security).
  • 專案直銷 (B2B/B2G)： 不走零售電商。鎖定印度陸軍、警察機關，及 Reliance 內部的電信塔巡檢與煉油廠安防。
  • Servitization (DaaS): Shifting clients from CapEx to OpEx. Clients subscribe to SkyDeck data analytics without buying the hardware.
  • 服務化轉型 (DaaS)： 大幅推動「無人機即服務」。客戶不買硬體，透過 SkyDeck 訂閱巡檢數據與 AI 分析。

Supply Chain & Logistics

供應鏈物流邏輯

  • Centralized Control: "High-value, low-volume" defense manufacturing model. Purchasing decisions strictly handled at Bengaluru HQ.
  • 中央集權： 「高價值、低頻次、高客製化」軍工生產邏輯。採購決策權 100% 集中於 Bengaluru 總部。
  • Compliance: Vendors must provide comprehensive Test Reports and material traceability certs.
  • 軍規合規： 供應商需具備提交完整產品測試報告 (Test Reports) 及材料溯源證明的能力。
