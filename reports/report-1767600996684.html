Foxlink India Electric Private Limited 正崴精密工業 (印度) 有限公司
OFFICIAL BUSINESS REPORT

MOM: Visit to Wangda Technologies Noida & Chennai Infrastructure

會議記錄：參訪 Wangda Technologies 諾依達與清奈工廠佈局

Objective: Establishing JDM/OEM Channels to Overcome Local Capacity Constraints

目標：建立 JDM/OEM 管道以突破印度在地化產能瓶頸

1. Background & Executive Summary 壹、 會議背景與執行摘要

The Challenge: Foxlink India currently faces a strategic bottleneck. Our business is heavily concentrated on wire/cable production for Apple and Vivo. We lack SMT, FATP, and Injection molding capabilities locally, and HQ has no immediate plans for new factory investment. This has led to substantial missed opportunities in the IoT and Automotive sectors.

現狀挑戰： 正崴印度目前面臨戰略瓶頸。現有業務高度集中於 Apple 與 Vivo 的線材生產，缺乏在地的 SMT、FATP 與注塑能力，且總部短期內無新廠投資計畫。這導致我們在物聯網（IoT）與汽車電子領域錯失了大量潛在訂單。

The Mission: This meeting was convened to evaluate Wangda Technologies as a potential "Back-end Manufacturing Partner." The goal is to establish a model where "Foxlink takes orders, Wangda handles production" to rapidly expand our product footprint in India with minimal capital risk.

任務目標： 本次會議旨在評估 Wangda Technologies 作為潛在「後端製造合作夥伴」的可行性。目標是建立「正崴接單、Wangda 生產」的合作模式，以最小的資金風險快速擴張我們在印度的產品版圖。

2. Key Discussion Items & Strategic Findings 貳、 核心討論事項與戰略發現

Item 1: Analysis of Wangda's Industrial Footprint

議題一：Wangda 工廠佈局與地理優勢分析

Wangda presented their 5-factory layout across India, demonstrating high flexibility for different logistical needs:

Wangda 展示了其在全印度的 5 座工廠佈局，展現了因應不同物流需求的極高靈活性：

  • North (Noida): 3 Factories focusing on SMT/FATP (Routers) and Injection. Proximity to Delhi Airport (41km) serves Northern clients like Samsung and Vivo.
  • 北方 (Noida)： 3 座工廠，專注於 SMT/FATP (路由器) 與注塑。鄰近德里機場 (41km)，服務 Samsung 與 Vivo 等北方客戶。
  • South (Chennai): 2 Factories with multi-modal logistics (Sea/Air). Located near industry giants like Foxconn and Pegatron, ensuring a mature supply chain cluster.
  • 南方 (Chennai)： 2 座工廠，具備海陸空聯運優勢。鄰近 Foxconn 與 Pegatron 等巨頭，確保成熟的供應鏈聚落。

Strategic Implication: Their Noida site has 500K/month idle FATP capacity. This is a critical asset that Foxlink can leverage immediately for new product launches without waiting for equipment lead times.

戰略意義： 其諾依達工廠擁有每月 50 萬件的閒置 FATP 產能。這對正崴來說是關鍵資產，可立即用於新產品發佈，無需等待漫長的設備交期。

在深度討論中，我們識別出三個正崴市場通路與 Wangda 產能高度匹配的切入點：

Item 3: Paradigm Shift - The GeM Portal Strategy

議題三：戰略轉型 - GeM 門戶策略

Wangda highlighted the Government e-Marketplace (GeM). This was a critical blind spot in our previous strategy.

Wangda 強調了政府電子市集 (GeM) 的重要性。這是我方先前戰略中的關鍵盲點。

The Logic: By manufacturing locally via Wangda and fulfilling the "Make in India" requirement, Foxlink can bid directly for Indian government contracts. This moves us from being a "Brand Vendor" (B2B) to a "Government Supplier" (B2G), accessing huge volumes and government subsidies.

核心邏輯： 透過 Wangda 在地製造並滿足「印度製造」要求，正崴可以直接投標印度政府標案。這使我們從「品牌供應商」(B2B) 轉型為「政府供應商」(B2G)，從而獲得龐大的採購量與政府補貼。
