5. 銷售與通路佈局

Channel Strategy

通路戰略概況

  • Dealer-led (3S): Over 100 touchpoints across India providing Sales, Service, and Spares. Concentrated in South/West India.
  • 經銷商模式 (3S)： 全印 100+ 觸點，高度集中於南部與西部，正向北印度擴張。
  • B2B Fleet Focus: "Bziness" series operates on a hybrid of leasing and direct procurement with last-mile delivery firms.
  • B2B 企業端： 「Bziness」系列與最後一哩路配送公司合作，採租賃與直購混合模式。
  • Digital Presence: Online test-ride bookings and fintech integrations for digital loan approvals on official site.
  • 數位平台： 官網直連，提供線上預約試乘與在線貸款審核服務。

Logistics Logic

供應鏈物流邏輯

  • Purchasing Hub: Centralized in Hyderabad. Vendors must have capability to dispatch directly to Pashamylaram Ind. Estate.
  • 採購重心： 位於海得拉巴。供應商需具備配送至 Pashamylaram 工業區的能力。
  • Aftermarket Network: Needs steady flow of spares to 100+ dealer service centers.
  • 售後網絡： 中央零件倉需穩定支援全印 100+ 經銷商的維修料件。
