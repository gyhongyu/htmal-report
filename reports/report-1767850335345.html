1. Strategic Context & Objective

1. 戰略背景與會議目標

The discussion represents a pivotal step in Nextron's strategy to enhance its "Made in India" product portfolio. The dialogue is driven by the Indian government's halt on new BIS certifications for Power Supply Units (PSUs), creating a time-sensitive imperative to localize production.

本次討論是 Nextron 強化其「印度製造」產品組合的關鍵一步。對話的主要驅動力來自印度政府近期暫停了新的電源供應器 (PSU) BIS 認證，這迫使 Nextron 必須盡快實現在地化生產以抓住市場機遇。

Primary Goal: 主要目標： Feasibility of manufacturing partnership for PSUs and SSDs. 探討 PSU 與 SSD 的製造合作可行性。
