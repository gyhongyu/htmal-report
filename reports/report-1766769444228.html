2. Discussion Record 貳、 會議討論內容

Item 1: Competitor Landscape - The "Capital Player" Insight

議題一：競爭格局分析 - 「資本玩家」與合資模式

Jeff Chen provided a deep-dive analysis of the top-tier local competitors, specifically identifying Minda, Uno Minda, and Lumax. He categorized these entities as "Capital Players" rather than technology innovators. Their business model relies almost exclusively on forming Joint Ventures (JVs) with international (primarily Japanese) firms to acquire manufacturing technology, rather than developing it in-house.

Jeff Chen 對印度本土頂級競爭對手進行了深度分析，特別點名了 Minda、Uno Minda 與 Lumax。他將這些企業歸類為「資本型玩家 (Capital Players)」，而非技術創新者。這些集團的商業模式高度依賴與國際大廠（主要是日商）成立合資企業 (JV) 來獲取製造技術，本身缺乏自主研發能力。

Strategic Implication for Foxlink: This confirms a gap in the market. While these incumbents have capital and connections, they lack the efficiency and yield rates of a dedicated manufacturer like Foxlink. Sentec's success in beating a Minda-Japanese JV (via higher yield/lower cost) proves that Foxlink can compete and win against these giants by offering superior manufacturing technology without the heavy overhead of a traditional JV structure. 對正崴的戰略意涵： 這確認了市場缺口的存在。雖然這些既有大廠擁有資金與人脈，但缺乏像 Foxlink 這樣專精製造廠的效率與良率。Sentec 先前擊敗 Minda 日系合資廠的案例（依靠高良率與低成本）證明了，Foxlink 若能提供優於傳統 JV 架構的製造技術與成本優勢，完全有能力從這些巨頭手中搶下市佔。

Item 2: Market Trends - GST Policy & Product Diversification

議題二：市場趨勢 - GST 稅收政策與產品多角化

Jeff highlighted a critical regulatory driver: The Indian government's GST structure heavily taxes engines over 350cc. This has forced OEMs to focus on the sub-350cc segment, incentivizing them to pack these smaller bikes with high-end features (digital clusters, connectivity) to maintain premium positioning.

Jeff 強調了一個關鍵的政策驅動力：印度政府的 GST 稅制對 350cc 以上的引擎課徵重稅。這迫使車廠（OEMs）將重心轉向 350cc 以下的級距，並致力於在這些車款中導入高階配備（如數位儀表、聯網功能）以維持產品的高端定位與利潤。

Sentec's Strategy: Despite holding ~70% market share in his core segment, Jeff considers it a "mature market." He is actively diversifying into "Air" components (e.g., intake systems) to drive growth. This signals that purely mechanical components are saturating, and the growth lies in electronics and new features—Foxlink's domain.

Sentec 的對應策略： 儘管 Jeff 在其核心領域擁有約 70% 的市佔率，但他認為該市場已屬「成熟市場」。他目前正積極多角化發展「空氣 (Air)」相關零組件（如進氣系統）以尋求成長。這訊號顯示純機械零件市場正在飽和，未來的增長點在於電子化與新功能——這正是 Foxlink 的強項。

Item 3: Foxlink Target Intelligence - Belrise Industries

議題三：Foxlink 目標情資 - Belrise Industries

Foxlink shared the upcoming plan to initiate partnership discussions with Belrise Industries in January. In response, Jeff shared significant intelligence: Belrise had previously approached Sentec for a potential Joint Venture.

我方提及預計一月份將與 Belrise Industries 展開合作洽談。對此，Jeff 分享了重要情報：Belrise 先前也曾接觸過 Sentec 尋求合資機會。

Jeff's Assessment & Warning: Jeff declined the partnership with Belrise for two key reasons:
1. No Market Share Gain: The JV would not help Sentec expand its share in the air cleaner/filter market.
2. Technology Risk: Jeff expressed strong concerns about the high risk of technology theft ("stolen technology") if they were to collaborate.

Jeff 的評估與警示： Jeff 當時拒絕了與 Belrise 的合作，主要基於兩點考量：
1. 無助於市佔擴張： 該合資案無法幫助 Sentec 在空氣濾清產品市場取得更多份額。
2. 技術風險： Jeff 強烈表達了對於技術被盜用 (Technology Theft) 的擔憂，認為合作存在高度風險。

Strategic Implication: As Foxlink proceeds with the January meeting, we must prioritize Intellectual Property (IP) protection mechanisms and treat technical disclosures with extreme caution.

戰略意涵： Foxlink 在推進一月份的會議時，必須將智慧財產權 (IP) 保護機制列為優先事項，並對技術資料的揭露採取極度謹慎的態度。

Item 4: Target Account Intelligence - Hero MotoCorp

議題四：目標客戶情資 - Hero MotoCorp 組織變動

Discussion revealed significant internal shifts at Hero MotoCorp. The company has recently undergone a major leadership overhaul, appointing a new CEO. This change has triggered the departure of many senior managers, which explains why Foxlink's existing contact list has become unresponsive.

討論揭露了 Hero MotoCorp 內部重大的變動。該公司近期進行了高層改組，任命了新任 CEO。此變動引發了許多資深經理人的離職，這也解釋了為何 Foxlink 現有的聯絡人清單近期回應率低落。

Furthermore, Hero is in the process of centralizing/relocating their Procurement and R&D centers. The exact new location is currently unconfirmed and requires immediate verification to realign our sales logistics.

此外，Hero 正在進行採購與研發 (R&D) 中心的集中化/搬遷。確切的新址目前尚未確認，這需要我們立即進行查證，以重新調整我們的業務拜訪動線。
