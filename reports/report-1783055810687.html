Supply Chain Logistics Logic

供應鏈物流邏輯

  • Transitioning to Dual Hubs: Currently, all finished vehicles dispatch from Hosur, TN. With Bidkin Factory 3.0 launching in mid-2026, a parallel Western India corridor is active.
  • 轉型雙核心： 目前整車均自泰米爾納德邦 Hosur 發往各地中轉倉。隨著馬哈拉施特拉邦新廠於 2026 年中啟用，將建立起西部供應鏈平行體系。
  • JIT Requirements: Electronics and wire harness suppliers must support JIT (Just-In-Time) deliveries simultaneously for Hosur and Aurangabad factories.
  • 即時制 (JIT) 需求： 其電控與精密電電子部件供應商必須具備「雙基地工廠供貨」及「即時 JIT 配送」之能力。
