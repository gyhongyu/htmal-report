Insight: The "Super Capacitor" Need

技術洞察：超級電容 (Super Capacitor) 成剛需

Due to India's severe summer heat, lithium-ion batteries in dash cams often swell or explode. Almost all brands are transitioning to Super Capacitors. Foxlink must ensure PCBA designs robustly integrate capacitor management.

因印度夏季高溫，傳統鋰電池行車記錄儀極易膨脹起火。市場已全面轉向「超級電容」。Foxlink 在爭取代工時，必須強調 PCBA 電源管理的設計與製造穩定性。

Insight: Connectivity Shift (4G/LTE)

趨勢洞察：4G 聯網型記錄儀崛起

Fleet owners and high-end users are demanding real-time cloud uploading (live view) to prevent theft. Next-gen PCBA will require 4G SIM module integration.

車隊管理與高階車主強烈需求「實時雲端監控」以防盜。下一代機種的 PCBA 必須整合 4G SIM 通訊模組，代工利潤將更高。
