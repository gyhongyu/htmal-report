1. Responses to Invest India's Inquiries 1. 針對來函詢問事項之回覆說明

Q1: Minimum Order Sizes & Indicative Investments

Q1: 最小訂單量門檻與對應的指示性資本投資

REVENUE & CAPEX 營收與資本支出

To ensure a robust and mutually beneficial investment, our indicative local CapEx planning aligns with three progressive revenue milestones ($5M / $15M / $40M USD). This ensures a risk-managed, structured build-up of local manufacturing capacity: starting with NPI sampling supported by our Vietnam factory, upgrading to SMT assembly lines in Tirupati once baseline volumes are achieved, and ultimately establishing the Noida wet process plant when long-term market demand is secured.

本公司秉持穩健經營原則，將印度本地的指示性資本支出（CapEx）與實際營收規模進行三階段科學化對照規劃（500萬 / 1,500萬 / 4,000萬美元）。透過循序漸進的模式，這使我們能以穩健的商業步伐建立本地產能：第一階段由越南提供打樣與驗證支援，第二階段於訂單規模達標後在 Tirupati 建置 SMT 生產線，第三階段則在長期市場規模確立後正式重啟 Noida 全製程廠房。

Q2: Vietnam Capacity Utilization & Buffer Setup

Q2: 越南廠產能利用率與專屬緩衝設定

SUPPLY CHAIN SECURITY 供應鏈安全

Our IATF 16949 certified Da Nang facility runs at an average of 75% to 80% utilization. Our dynamic planning allocates the remaining 20% to 25% capacity buffer (up to 50,000 m²) as a dedicated buffer for our Indian OEM clients. This buffer perfectly absorbs Phase 1 (NPI) sampling and guarantees steady, secure bare board supply for Phase 2 SMT assembly in Tirupati.

本公司已獲車規級 IATF 16949 認證的越南峴港廠平均利用率目前為 75%~80%。我們特別為印度客戶規劃了佔該廠總產能 20%~25%（最高可達年產 50,000 平方米）的專屬緩衝產能。此配置不僅能 100% 確保第一階段 NPI 打樣與技術驗證的快速交期，亦能穩健支應第二階段 SMT 組裝所需的裸板進口，充分保障供應鏈安全。

Q3: Strategic Stance on Joint Ventures (JV)

Q3: 對於印度設立合資公司 (JV) 的戰略立場

Data-Driven Approach (Evaluation First): While we remain open to exploring a Joint Venture (JV) partnership in India with a suitable partner, any decision must be strictly based on empirical market research data. Without sufficient hard data to support the Return on Investment (ROI) evaluation, it is extremely difficult for our Group Board to make any investment decisions, even if potential JV partners are identified. Therefore, we suggest postponing formal discussions regarding a concrete JV framework to the next stage, and urge Invest India to coordinate the release of India Customs Data for the past 3 years (please refer to the List of FPC-related HS Codes). Real historical data is the key driver to unlock board approval.

數據驅動評估 (常規路徑)：本公司對在印度尋找合適夥伴成立合資公司 (JV) 持開放態度，但決策必須以實質的「市場調研數據」為依據。若沒有足夠的硬數據來支持投資回報率 (ROI) 的評估，即便有潛在的合資人選，總部董事會也極難做出投資決策。因此，我們建議將具體的合資框架討論推遲至下一階段。並促請 Invest India 協調提供印度近三年海關進口數據（請點此查看 👉 FPC 相關 HS Code 清單）。有真實進口數據做為底氣，總部董事會才有可能放行投資決策。

Strategic Fast-Track (Executive-Level Collaboration): If Invest India already holds locked-in demands or commitments from domestic anchors (e.g. Tata Group, Ola Electric) that guarantee immediate profitability for a local joint venture, this transcends regional business calculations. In this scenario, we will facilitate a fast-track channel to present a joint strategic proposal directly to our Group General Manager for a top-down decision.

戰略快速通道 (高層戰略對接)：若 Invest India 手中已掌握印度本土龍頭企業（如 Tata、Ola）對高階 FPC 軟板的剛性採購承諾，一旦成立 JV 可立竿見影實現獲利。這已超越常規區域業務評估，屬於集團最高層級合作。我們將協助開闢綠色通道，直接向本集團**總經理**遞交聯合計畫案。

3. Backing Capacity Analysis 3. 後端支援產能分析

Da Nang (Vietnam) Factory Capacity Matching

越南峴港廠產能匹配驗證

Our Da Nang (Vietnam) facility has an annual capacity of 250,000 m². The current average utilization rate is 80% (200,000 m²). The remaining 20% (50,000 m²) is fully dedicated as an India-exclusive buffer to support Phase 1 (NPI) and supply Phase 2 (SMT) FPC bare boards, ensuring complete supply security.
