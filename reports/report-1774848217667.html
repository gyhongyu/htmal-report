5. Sales & Channel Strategy

5. 銷售與通路佈局

Channel Strategy

通路策略概況

  • 100% B2B Direct-to-OEM: No middlemen. Highly customized Tier-1 products sold directly to automakers via long-term contracts.
  • 純 B2B 直銷模式： 車用線束為高度客製化 Tier-1 產品，無中間商，直接與 OEM 簽署長期合約。
  • Core Client Roster: Maruti Suzuki (MSIL), Tata Motors, Mahindra & Mahindra, Hyundai, Toyota.
  • 核心客戶群： Maruti Suzuki, Tata Motors, Mahindra, Hyundai, Toyota 等印度主力車廠。
  • Zero E-Commerce: Not applicable for retail B2B/B2C platforms. Purely enterprise connections.
  • 數位平台： 無公開零售電商佈局，純企業級直連對接。

Supply Chain & Logistics Logic

供應鏈物流邏輯

  • Just-In-Time (JIT) Delivery: Harnesses are bulky "Sequenced Parts". Aptiv sets up warehouses within kilometers of OEM assembly lines.
  • JIT 即時交付： 線束體積大且為車輛組裝排序件。Aptiv 於車廠總裝線數公里內設有供貨倉。
  • Vendor Mandate: Upstream connector/terminal vendors must ensure absolute lead-time stability and maintain local buffer stock.
  • 上游要求： 要求其連接器與端子供應商具備極高交期穩定度，並需建立在地安全庫存 (Buffer Stock)。
