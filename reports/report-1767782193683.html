2. Discussion Record 貳、 會議討論內容

Item 1: Cattle Device - Strategic Order Consolidation

議題一：牛隻追蹤設備 - 策略性訂單整合

Context: The original plan was a 2-phase pilot (10 units then 70). Client now proposed moving directly to a single 80-unit order.

背景： 原計畫為兩階段試點（10 台後接 70 台）。客戶現在提議直接整合為單次 80 台的訂單。

Detail: Client expresses high confidence in core modules (GPS/GSM/Health). They requested adding "Activity Tracking" (IMU sensors) to this batch. They formally committed to accepting the 80 units even if the new activity feature requires further calibration, protecting Foxlink from hardware liability.

細節： 客戶對核心模組（GPS/GSM/健康監測）表現出高度信心，並要求在此批次中加入「活動追蹤」（IMU 感測器）。客戶正式承諾，即便新功能需進一步校準，也會接收全數 80 台設備，確保正崴免於硬體賠償風險。

Implication: This accelerates the production cycle and consolidates procurement, indicating the client's aggressive push for market deployment.

戰略意義： 此舉加速了生產週期並整合了採購流程，顯示客戶對市場部署的激進態度。

Item 2: Commercial Deadlock - "TT in Advance" Requirement

議題二：商務僵局 - 「電匯預付」要求

Context: Foxlink standards require full upfront payment (TT) for initial material procurement.

背景： 根據正崴標準流程，初期材料採購需全額預付（TT）。

Detail: Client expressed serious concerns, as they previously understood a "test-then-pay" model. A late shift to TT requires their internal finance re-approval, potentially delaying the timeline. Client offered a "future volume commitment" in exchange for a payment exception.

細節： 客戶表示嚴重關切，因先前理解為「先測試後付款」。此時轉向預付模式需其內部財務重新核准，恐延誤進度。客戶提出以「未來量產承諾」換取付款條件的例外處理。

Implication: If Foxlink management does not grant an exception, the project launch may be pushed back by several weeks due to client's internal bureaucracy.

戰略意義： 若正崴管理層不給予例外許可，專案啟動可能因客戶內部流程延誤數週。

Item 3: Pet Tracker - Technical Pivot to 4G

議題三：寵物追蹤器 - 轉向 4G 的技術轉型

Context: 2G samples were found incompatible with the Indian network (hardcoded for China).

背景： 經發現，原 2G 樣品與印度網路不相容（僅針對中國市場硬寫入）。

Detail: Foxlink proposed a full 4G redesign with an NRE cost of 70,000 RMB (~9 Lakhs INR). To mitigate this cost, Foxlink proposed a rebate model where the NRE is amortized or discounted against future high-volume orders.

細節： 正崴提議進行全面的 4G 重新設計，NRE 費用為 7 萬人民幣（約 90 萬盧比）。為緩解此成本壓力，正崴提出返利模式，即 NRE 費用可在未來的大量訂單中抵扣或折讓。

Implication: Ensures long-term technical viability in India but requires the client to commit to a multi-year volume forecast to justify the investment.

戰略意義： 確保了在印度的長期技術可行性，但要求客戶必須提供多年期產量預測以支持此項投資。
