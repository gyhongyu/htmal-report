Direct Direct B2B & DaaS Model

直銷與無人機即服務 (DaaS)

  • Solution Selling: 90%+ turnover is generated via direct enterprise solutions and state government tenders. Highly structured.
  • 解決方案包裝： 90% 以上營收來自直銷與政府招標。以「飛手操作 + 硬體租賃 + 自研 SaaS」端到端交付。
  • Zero Retail Footprint: Fully verified. No Amazon India or Flipkart consumer sales pipelines exist due to the professional nature of commercial drone tech.
  • 排除消費零售： 已全面驗證。Amazon India 及 Flipkart 無任何零售管道，屬於純企業/政府級重工業產品。
  • Govt & Private Giants: Major vendor for SVAMITVA village mapping; long-term service contracts with Tata Steel, Coal India, and Adani.
  • 標杆客戶群： 國家 SVAMITVA 計畫最大中標商之一；私營大廠涵蓋 Tata Steel、Coal India、Adani 礦區 3D 立體數字化工程。

Logistics & Compliance Tracking

物流、防偽與一機一碼合規

  • DGCA UIN Mandate: Strictly regulated by DGCA. Every individual drone is assigned a Unique Identification Number (UIN) prior to dispatch.
  • 民航局 UIN 合規： 受到極嚴格監管，出廠每架飛機需取得專屬 UIN（Unique Identification Number）防偽標記溯源。
  • Direct HQ Delivery: All hardware packages are distributed directly from Jakkur, Bengaluru HQ directly to designated mines or land survey projects.
  • 總部直供現場： 所有硬體整機均由班加羅爾 Jakkur 總部直發客戶現場或政府項目點。
