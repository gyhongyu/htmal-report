2. Discussion Record 貳、 會議討論內容

Item 1: Foxlink's "China Plus One" Strategy & India Assets

議題一：正崴「中國加一」戰略與印度資產佈局

Context: To align with global customer mandates (e.g., Apple), Foxlink is aggressively shifting manufacturing capabilities to India.

背景： 為符合全球客戶（如 Apple）的策略要求，正崴正積極將生產能力轉移至印度。

Detail: While the Tirupati plant is active (shifting to C2C and SSD modules), the 25,000 sqm Chennai facility is ready but "on hold" per HQ policy which forbids activation without confirmed business orders.

細節： 目前 Tirupati 廠營運中（轉產 C2C 線材與 SSD 模組）；清奈 25,000 平方米新廠已完工，但受限於總部「無訂單不開廠」的政策，目前處於停工待命狀態。

Implication: Securing a partnership with Sanmina is critical to providing the necessary "business case" to HQ for activating the Chennai plant and transferring equipment/tech from China.

影響： 與 Sanmina 建立合作關係，是向總部提出啟動清奈廠及從中國/台灣移轉設備與技術之「商業計畫」的關鍵關鍵。

Item 2: Sanmina’s High-Complexity Infrastructure in Chennai

議題二：Sanmina 在清奈的高複雜度製造基礎設施

Context: Sanmina positions itself as a Tier-1 EMS provider for mission-critical industries (Medical, Defense, Cloud).

背景： Sanmina 定位為一級 EMS 服務商，專注於任務關鍵型產業（醫療、國防、雲端）。

Detail: Their Chennai campus spans 100 acres with 20 SMT lines, operating as a JV with Reliance (STS). They recently acquired Zi system for AI server manufacturing (AMD partner).

細節： 其清奈園區佔地 100 英畝，擁有 20 條 SMT 線，與 Reliance 合資成立 STS。近期併購 Zi system 以切入 AI 伺服器製造（AMD 合作夥伴）。

Implication: Sanmina’s focus on low-to-mid volume/high-complexity products complements Foxlink’s component expertise, offering a pathway for high-value localized sourcing.

影響： Sanmina 專注於中低產量/高複雜度產品，與正崴的零組件專業互補，為高價值在地化採購提供了路徑。

Item 3: Collaborative "Made in India" Advocacy

議題三：「印度製造」協作模式提議

Context: Bridging the gap between Foxlink's idle capacity and Sanmina’s current Chinese sourcing reliance.

背景： 旨在彌合正崴閒置產能與 Sanmina 目前對中國採購依賴之間的差距。

Detail: Foxlink proposed that Sanmina issue RFQs for components currently imported (Connectors, EV BMS, Component Trays). Foxlink will use these RFQs to justify transferring production lines from China to India.

細節： 我方建議 Sanmina 針對目前從中國進口的零組件（連接器、電動車 BMS、吸塑托盤）發出詢價單 (RFQ)。正崴將以此作為向總部爭取將產線從中國轉移至印度的依據。

Implication: This "business-first" approach enables both companies to bypass government marketing inertia and build a tangible local supply chain.

影響： 這種「業務優先」的方法使兩家公司能夠繞過政府機關的行政推延，建立實質的在地供應鏈。
