FOXLINK Opportunity 1: AS 9100D Connectors & Wiring Harness

正崴機遇 1: AS 9100D 連接器與軍工線束切入

Logic Fruit’s OpenVPX VPX computer boards handle high shock, vibration, and dust defense environments. They aggressively need ruggedized I/O interfaces. Foxlink’s heavy-duty high-speed interconnects and specialized cabling assemblies can perfectly resolve their hardware interconnect weaknesses.

Logic Fruit 在古爾岡研發的大量加固型 OpenVPX 單板電腦主要面向高抗震、防塵的嚴苛軍工環境，急需引入具有高階防護的射頻連接器與軍工級線束製造技術。正崴在高頻高速傳輸線束與精密連接器的整合能力，能完美切入其板卡組裝供應鏈。

FOXLINK Opportunity 2: High-Layer PCB & Localized EMS/ODM

正崴機遇 2: 12-24層高階 PCB 印度本土代工與 EMS

Under India's strict local sourcing guidelines ("Make in India"), Logic Fruit needs to migrate all its imported board designs (12-24 layer PCBs) and complex high-frequency generators to local production. Foxlink can leverage its India setup as a premier manufacturing vendor for localized PCBA & EMS.

在印度國防部 "Make in India" 的 100% 國產化採購壓力下，Logic Fruit 急需將其高頻板卡、射頻發生器板卡（12-24層HDI PCB）從「進口後組裝」改為「完全印度本土生產」。正崴在印度在地的先進產線可成為其幕後高階 PCB 與 EMS 代工首選。
