5. Sales & Channel Strategy

5. 銷售與通路佈局分析

Go-to-Market Model

通路策略概況 (Go-to-Market)

  • B2B/B2G Direct Bidding: Direct sales and delivery to Ministry of Defence (MoD), Agri boards, and heavy infra giants (like L&T).
  • B2B/B2G 專案直銷： 針對印度國防部 (MoD)、基建公司及政府標案，採直接競標交機。
  • Micro-Franchise (DaaS): Instead of selling hardware directly to farmers, they setup Custom Hiring Centers (CHC). Franchises buy drones to offer "spraying services".
  • 微型加盟與 DaaS： 推廣 CHC 經銷加盟制。加盟主購買無人機後，提供當地農民「代客噴灑」服務。
  • Digital Independence: Zero reliance on Amazon/B2C platforms. Heavy lead generation via official website (Dealer & Academy portals).
  • 直營數位平台： 不依賴消費性電商平台，透過官網完整系統進行經銷商與飛手培訓註冊。

Logistics Logic

供應鏈物流邏輯

  • Hub & Spoke Dispatch: Finished hardware is dispatched centrally from the Chennai Plant radiating outward to CHCs and Service Centers across states.
  • 中央輻射發貨： 憑藉各邦服務站，硬體由 Chennai 中央工廠輻射發貨至各地 CHC。
  • Vendor Mandate: Component suppliers MUST have robust logistics capabilities to ensure stable, JIT delivery to the main Chennai warehouse.
  • 供應鏈要求： 供應商需具備穩定交貨至清奈總倉的運籌能力，不可斷鏈。
