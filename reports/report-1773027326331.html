1. Core Strategy & Principles 1. 核心策略與指導原則

The "Evidence" Rule

「證據力」原則

Show Proof of Effort

展示盡職努力證明

When hardware compliance is impossible, we must provide documented proof that Foxlink tried. Government rejection letters or landlord notices are mandatory.

當硬體無法合規時，必須提供書面證據證明我們已盡力。如政府拒絕信、發給房東的存證信函等。

The "Mitigation" Rule

「補償措施」原則

Alternative Controls 實施替代管控

If the landlord refuses to upgrade fire systems, we double the manual patrols and fire extinguishers to lower the immediate risk level.

若房東拒絕升級消防系統，我們必須增加兩倍的人工巡檢與滅火器，以實質降低當下風險等級。

The "Client Waiver"

「客戶豁免」原則

DELL is the Final Judge

客戶擁有最終裁量權

RBA scores may drop, but detailed CAPs submitted via the RBA Portal allow DELL's Compliance Officers to grant us operational waivers.

RBA 系統分數可能會低，但只要在系統中提交詳盡的 CAP，DELL 合規官即可依此給予我們接單豁免。

Scenario A: Lack of Industrial Wastewater Infrastructure

場景 A：缺乏工業級汙廢水處理設施 / 無法挖管

Issue: Facility is leased, not in an industrial park, local govt denies pipe connection.

狀況描述：承租廠房非專業工業區，政府拒絕開挖道路連接公用管網。

CAP Field CAP 系統欄位 Approved English Response (Copy & Paste) 官方核准英文回覆 (可直接複製)
Root Cause

The facility is a leased property located in an area currently lacking municipal industrial wastewater infrastructure. Applications to construct external pipelines were rejected by local authorities due to zoning constraints.

(說明受限於當地基礎設施與政府法規)
Correction (Immediate)

We have contracted a government-authorized third-party hazardous/wastewater management vendor to collect and dispose of the wastewater regularly. Zero discharge to local environment.

(立即作為：聘請合法清運商，保證零外排)
Corrective Action (Long-term)

1. Maintain valid contracts with the authorized vendor. 2. Implement a small-scale ZLD (Zero Liquid Discharge) system for internal recycling by Q4 2026. 3. Engage with the landlord/local government bi-annually to check for infrastructure updates.

(長期計畫：評估 ZLD 系統、定期發函追蹤基礎建設進度)

Scenario B: Landlord Refuses Fire Safety Upgrades

場景 B：房東拒絕配合消防系統硬體升級

Issue: Building requires structural fire upgrades, but landlord ignores requests.

狀況描述：建築結構或中央消防需要升級，但房東不願出資或不回應。

CAP Field CAP 系統欄位 Approved English Response (Copy & Paste) 官方核准英文回覆 (可直接複製)
Root Cause

As per the lease agreement, structural fire safety modifications fall under the landlord's jurisdiction. Despite multiple formal requests and legal notices, the landlord has not initiated the required upgrades.

(說明合約限制，且房東對正式催告不予理會)
Correction (Immediate)

We have implemented robust compensatory measures: Installed standalone smoke detectors across the floor, increased portable fire extinguishers by 50% above legal requirement, and established an internal 24/7 fire-watch patrol team.

(立即作為：安裝獨立警報器、增加滅火器、建立 24 小時巡邏隊)
Corrective Action (Long-term)

1. Issue quarterly formal legal notices to the landlord. 2. Conduct monthly emergency evacuation drills (exceeding RBA requirements). 3. Senior management will review relocation options upon lease expiration if the issue persists.

(長期計畫：按季寄發存證信函、增加逃生演練頻率、評估搬廠)

3. Mandatory Evidence & Client Communication

3. 必備證據清單與客戶溝通策略

Required Uploads (RBA Portal)

系統必須上傳之附件清單

  • Official Denials: Letters from Govt/Municipality denying construction permits.
  • 官方拒絕函： 當地政府拒絕核發施工許可或管網銜接的公文。
  • Legal Notices: Registered mail/Lawyer notices sent to the landlord.
  • 存證信函： 透過律師發送給房東要求整改的正式函件。
  • Alternative Contracts: Valid agreements with waste disposal agencies (translated to English).
  • 替代合約： 與合法廢棄物清運商簽訂的有效合約 (需附英文翻譯)。
  • Mitigation Logs: Photos of extra fire extinguishers, sign-in sheets of fire patrols.
  • 補償措施紀錄： 新增滅火器之照片、消防巡邏簽到表。

Offline Communication with DELL

與 DELL 客戶的線下溝通策略

  • Do not wait for the audit report. Proactively inform DELL's Compliance Lead about infrastructure limitations.
  • 切勿被動等待報告： 應主動致信 DELL 合規主管，說明當地硬體限制。
  • Request Leverage: In cases of landlord dispute, ask DELL procurement to issue a formal request to the landlord (Brand power works in India).
  • 請求品牌施壓： 針對房東不配合，可請 DELL 採購端出面發函給房東 (品牌影響力在印度非常有效)。
  • Seek Waivers: Present the uploaded CAP and explicitly request an operational waiver to proceed with D1 project onboarding.
  • 申請豁免： 呈報已提交之 CAP，明確要求給予營運豁免，以確保 D1 專案如期導入。
