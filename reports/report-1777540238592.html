Foxlink Opportunity / Gaps
供應鏈缺口與 Foxlink 機會點

Server-Grade Shift: AAEON's AI Box PCs now require thermal and interconnect standards close to enterprise servers. Foxlink's telecom/server connectivity expertise is a perfect match.

伺服器規格下放： 研揚 Edge AI 產品線內部需要大量耐高溫、抗震的高階連接器與傳輸線，規格逼近伺服器等級。

Global Scale & Local Customization: Needs vendors capable of passing strict ASUS QA audits while supporting high-mix industrial runs.

高階線纜切入： Foxlink 若能利用通訊研發優勢，提供高性價比的工業互連方案，可有效打入其 AVL 名單。
