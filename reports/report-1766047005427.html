1. Executive Summary: The Strategy

壹、 執行摘要：核心戰略

Syrma SGS is one of India's fastest-growing EMS companies (Listed), aggressively leveraging the PLI scheme. While they excel in PCBA, their strategic bottleneck is the reliance on imported electromechanical components (Connectors, Cables) for their "Box Build" ambition. They are actively seeking to localize their supply chain to improve margins and speed.

Syrma SGS 是印度成長最快的上市 EMS 公司之一，正積極利用 PLI 補貼擴張。雖然他們在 PCBA 領域表現卓越，但其轉型「整機組裝 (Box Build)」的戰略瓶頸在於高度依賴進口的機電元件（連接器、線束）。他們正積極尋求供應鏈在地化，以提高利潤率並縮短交期。

Strategic Pitch: 核心話術：

"Foxlink isn't just a vendor; we are your localized Tier-2 powerhouse. We can supply the critical connectors and cable assemblies you currently import, enabling you to claim higher local value addition (PLI) for your automotive and industrial clients."

「正崴不只是供應商，我們是您在地化的 Tier-2 強力後盾。我們能供應您目前仰賴進口的關鍵連接器與線束，助您在車用與工控客戶端獲取更高的在地附加價值 (符合 PLI 標準)。」
