2. Discussion Record 貳、 會議討論內容

Item 1: Strategic Alliance Shift & Bosch's "Internal Foundry" Model

議題一：策略聯盟轉向與 Bosch 的「內部生產基地」模式

Context: Bosch's Mobility business is a major target, accounting for over 70% of its global revenue, but its supply chain is notoriously closed.

背景： Bosch 移動出行業務佔其全球營收 70% 以上，為必爭之策略帳戶，但其供應鏈極度封閉且門檻嚴苛。

Detail: The Naganathapura plant operates as an "Internal Foundry" serving four internal business divisions (Power Solutions, Cross-Domain Computing, Vehicle Motion, 2WP) rather than direct end-market customers.

細節： Bosch Naganathapura 廠定位為「內部生產基地 (Internal Foundry)」，負責支撐內部四大業務單位（動力系統、跨域計算、制動懸掛、兩輪車電子），而非直接面對外部終端市場。

Implication: Consequently, Foxlink must transition from a traditional parts supplier to a deep localization partner to align with Bosch's internal operational demands and successfully bypass strict Tier-1 entry barriers.

影響： 因此，正崴必須從單純的零件供應商，轉型為深度在地化合作夥伴，以符合其內部採購需求並繞過嚴苛的准入門檻。

Item 2: Strict Localization Threshold ("Made in India")

議題二：嚴格的在地化門檻 (Made in India)

Context: Driven by high anti-dumping duties and localization commitments to OEMs (e.g., Tata, Mahindra), Bosch's procurement strategy has radically shifted.

背景： 受限於印度政府高額的反傾銷稅，以及對 OEM 客戶（如 Tata, Mahindra）的國產化承諾，Bosch 採購策略發生了巨變。

Mr. Suprith explicitly stated: "If you cannot do local production (Made in India), I will throw your proposal in the trash."

Suprith 先生明確警告：「若無法做到在地化生產 (Made in India)，我會直接將你的提案丟進垃圾桶。」

Detail & Implication: Bosch no longer accepts pure imported component quotes. Price is no longer the sole battleground; the core competitive challenge is leveraging our alliance to bypass the 50% localization verification threshold and tariff burdens by providing a complete, localized value chain.

細節與影響： 目前 Bosch 已完全不接受單純進口零件報價。競爭的核心已非單價，而是正崴如何透過策略聯盟，提供完整的在地產能價值鏈，成功繞過 50% 的驗證門檻與關稅重負。

Item 3: The "Joint Fleet" Value Proposition (Delta, Foxlink, Pavone)

議題三：「聯合艦隊」價值主張 (台達、正崴、Pavone)

Context: Single-supplier models fail to meet Bosch's risk and security requirements, necessitating a consolidated, one-stop approach.

背景： 單打獨鬥的供應模式已無法滿足 Bosch 的安全性與規模要求，必須提供一站式的解決方案。

Detail: We presented a tripartite alliance:

細節： 我們向客戶展示了三方聯合艦隊的實力：

  • Delta Electronics: Offers 16 SMT lines and a 20-acre "Supplier Park" for JIT delivery. Crucially, Delta already holds Bosch/BSH green-list supplier status.
  • 台達電子 (Delta)： 提供 16 條 SMT 自動化產線及 20 英畝「供應商園區」以實現 JIT 近距離交付，且具備 Bosch/BSH 綠色供應商資格的信任背書。
  • Foxlink: Injects critical precision components (e.g., DCDC connectors, FPCs) via systematic technology transfer from Vietnam to India.
  • 正崴精密 (Foxlink)： 透過越南或中國廠區的技術轉移，填補 BOM 中在地化精密組件 (如 DCDC 連接器、FPC) 的缺口。
  • Pavone Tech: Accelerates AIS/BIS technical certifications and acts as a Customs House Agent (CHA) to resolve logistics friction.
  • Pavone Technologies： 負責 AIS/BIS/WPC 等技術認證，並具備報關行 (CHA) 身份，解決清關效率與法規合規性。

Implication: This unified model effectively mitigates Bosch's core anxieties regarding capacity expansion (CAPEX) risks, anti-dumping margin erosion, and lengthy 1-2 year audit cycles.

影響： 此模式完美解決了客戶在擴廠 CAPEX 風險、高額反傾銷稅侵蝕利潤，以及冗長 Tier 1 稽核週期上的核心痛點。

Item 4: Specific Product Line Requirements & Technical Boundaries

議題四：關鍵產品線需求與技術邊界

Context: Clarifying the exact technical scope for Foxlink's R&D allocation to align with Bosch's internal engineering practices.

背景： 需釐清 Bosch 的具體技術需求，以精確引導正崴後續的研發資源投入方向。

Detail:

細節：

  • 10.1" LCD Modules: Bosch procures only the glass/module. Internal control PCBAs are designed and assembled in-house by Bosch.
  • 10.1 吋 LCD 顯示屏： Bosch 僅採購面板模組 (Glass/Module)，內部驅動控制 PCBA 由其自製。
  • EV Solutions (2W/3W): The focus is on integrated "Charger Plus Converter" solutions outside of traditional BMS.
  • 電動車 (EV) 方案： 聚焦兩輪/三輪車的 Charger Plus Converter 整合型充電方案。
  • DCDC Connectors: Identified as the most urgent item for import substitution.
  • DCDC 連接器： 為目前進口替代最急迫的項目，亟需落實印度製造。
  • Automation & Testing: Bosch's final assembly is semi-automated; they highly prefer partners to develop custom Automated Test Equipment (ATE) jigs.
  • 自動化測試： 最終組裝為半自動化 (Semi-auto)，對專屬自動化測試設備 (ATE) 治具開發需求極高。

Implication: As Foxlink India does not have local R&D, Delta will lead and showcase its track record in control circuits and EV solutions (e.g., leveraging TVS experience). Foxlink will focus on cooperating in optoelectronic integration and preparing engineering support for custom ATE jigs.

影響： 由於正崴印度無在地研發團隊，控制電路及 EV 方案（如 TVS 經驗對標）將由台達 (Delta) 展現實績並主導開發；正崴則專注配合光電整合，並籌備針對其產線的專屬治具工程支援。
