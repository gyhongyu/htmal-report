B2B Co-Engineering Strategy

B2B 同步工程配套模式

  • Pure B2B OEM-First: Direct cooperation with leading passenger car and two-wheeler OEMs. Integrated into the early-stage "Simultaneous Engineering" vehicle design phases to offer custom optical solutions.
  • 純 B2B 深度配套： 直接對接各大主流整車廠的研發與採購部門。在整車開發初期的「同步工程」即切入，提供專用車型定製化照明方案。
  • Synergy in Aftermarket: For spare auto lighting markets, Lumax Industries routes finished goods directly to sister group company Lumax Auto Technologies Limited's (LATL) "Aftermarket Division", utilizing their nationwide network rather than selling directly to consumer retail stores.
  • 集團售後事業聯動： 在售後市場，Lumax Industries 自身的成品燈具會直接供貨給其集團兄弟公司 Lumax Auto Technologies 旗下的「售後市場分銷部」，自身不直接對接終端零售。
  • B2B Digital Portal: Official domain is https://www.lumaxworld.in/lumaxindustries. Purely designed for B2B capability marketing; no direct B2C e-commerce stores (like Amazon India or Flipkart) as product fits are heavy tier-1 subassemblies.
  • 數位官網宣傳： 官方網站為 https://www.lumaxworld.in/lumaxindustries。作為核心技術展示與 B2B 客戶關係入口，無直接的零售電商直營。

Just-In-Time Logistics Flow

Just-In-Time 零庫存物流邏輯

  • OEM-Adjacent Warehouses: Region Dispatch Centers (RDCs) in Bawal, Gurgaon, Pune, Chennai, etc. are located highly adjacent to major clients (Maruti Suzuki, Tata, Mahindra, Hyundai, Kia) to enable high-frequency Just-In-Time (JIT) sequence deliveries.
  • 緊貼 OEM 倉儲： 在四大汽車產業聚落均設有配送中心 (RDC)，與 Maruti Suzuki (Gurugram)、M&M (Pune)、Hyundai (Chennai) 等主力客戶緊密咬合，完全採取「JIT」即時交貨。
  • Aftermarket Reach: Backed by the Jain Group's massive aftermarket retail setup, Lumax components flow into over 300+ authorized distributors and 20,000+ automotive part retailers across India for vehicle repair.
  • 售後配件覆蓋： 依靠集團全印售後網路，對接超過 300 家授權分銷商、20,000+ 個零售汽配通路，提供原廠規格的車燈替換件。
