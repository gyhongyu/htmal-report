5. Sales & Channel Strategy

5. 銷售與通路佈局

Sales Approach

銷售模式

  • B2B OEM (Core): Direct tie-ups with auto majors (Mahindra, Tata, Maruti). Entering new EV platforms at early design stages as a Tier-1 giant.
  • B2B OEM 直供 (核心)： 與車廠 (Mahindra, Tata, Maruti) 直接簽約，以 Tier-1 身份介入新電動車型早期開發。
  • Aftermarket: Spares handled by independent entity "Valeo Service India".
  • B2B 售後市場： 透過獨立實體 "Valeo Service India" 經營。
  • Digital Focus: No B2C presence. Heavily invests in "Valeo TechAssist"—an online catalog & troubleshooting portal for mechanics/garages.
  • 數位平台： 無 B2C 電商。主推 "Valeo TechAssist" 針對修車廠提供技術支援與零件目錄。

Logistics Logic

供應鏈物流邏輯

  • "Plant-in-Plant" Setup: Units are built hyper-close to OEM clients (e.g., Sanand facility next to Tata, Pune next to Mahindra).
  • 廠區伴生 (Plant-in-Plant)： 廠區極度靠近 OEM 客戶 (例如 Sanand 鄰近 Tata，Pune 鄰近 Mahindra)。
  • VMI Mandate: Due to extreme proximity needs, suppliers to Valeo must set up VMI (Vendor Managed Inventory) warehouses to ensure Just-In-Time (JIT) dispatch.
  • VMI 供應鏈： 要求其供應商必須在汽車重鎮設立 VMI 倉庫，以實現嚴格的 JIT 交貨。
