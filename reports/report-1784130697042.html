5. 銷售與通路佈局分析

Direct B2B & Aftermarket Integration

直銷 B2B 與售後網絡整合

  • Direct-to-OEM (B2B): Highly customized safety assets. Directly integrating engineers into key automotive OEM hubs (Pune, Chennai).
  • 直接對接 OEM (B2B): 高度定制與高危安全產品。銷售模式完全為 B2B 工程直銷，直接對接乘用車、商用車及 2W/3W 廠。
  • Enterprise Bulk Contracts: Direct pricing and delivery contracts with telecom infra leaders (Indus Towers) and state grid storage developers.
  • 大宗採購協議: 與電信基礎設施巨頭（如 Indus Towers）及大型電網儲能承包商簽署長期直供合同。
  • Aftermarket Integration: Preparing to utilize the parent group's massive network of 150,000+ lead-acid distributors and service points for replacement and generic backup cells.
  • 售後體系複用: 未來針對通用儲能或售後市場，計劃複用母公司 Exide Industries 遍布全印的 150,000+ 傳統網點提供維修與銷售。
  • No Consumer E-Commerce: Zero direct presence on B2C e-commerce like Amazon/Flipkart due to safety regulations and pure industrial transaction scale.
  • 無電商覆蓋: 因屬於高度重型、高安規要求的工業品，在 Amazon 或 Flipkart 等 B2C 平台無任何直銷渠道。

Logistics Logic

供應鏈物流邏輯

  • Bangalore Core Hub: Batteries go through chemical aging/testing at Devanahalli. Dispatched globally using shock-absorbent climate-controlled specialized logistics.
  • 班加羅爾核心化成: 電芯在 Devanahalli 完成分容化成後，使用防震控溫專用集裝箱發貨。
  • Prantij Pack Assembly JIT: Cells are dispatched to Gujarat Prantij unit for localized module/pack housing, aligned closely with OEM JIT systems.
  • 古吉拉特工廠 JIT 響應: 部分電芯送至 Prantij 組裝成電池包，與終端車廠產線 JIT 深度綁定。
