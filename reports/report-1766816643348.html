2. Discussion Record 貳、 會議討論內容

Item 1: Operational Crisis - The Component Bottleneck

議題一：營運痛點 - 電子原器件供應瓶頸

Problem Statement: Foxlink India currently lacks a reliable local ecosystem for power and electronic components. Quotation requests (RFQ) often face delays of weeks or even months, causing many potential projects to be aborted ("stillborn").

問題陳述： 正崴印度目前缺乏穩定的電源與電子原器件在地供應鏈。報價請求 (RFQ) 常面臨數週甚至數月的延遲，導致許多潛在專案因此「胎死腹中」。

Objective with Pionex: Satish was invited specifically for his cross-border capabilities. He operates effectively in both China and India, possessing established channels for electronic components and strong connections with Indian Customs/Imports. The goal is to onboard him as a strategic supplier to drastically reduce quotation turnaround time.

合作目的： 邀請 Satish 的主因在於其跨境調度能力。他長期往返中印，熟悉電子業生態並掌握海關人脈。目標是將其納入合格供應商，解決報價時效過長的致命傷。

Item 2: Market Reality - Boat & Noise (The "Trader" Mindset)

議題二：市場真相 - Boat 與 Noise 的「貿易商屬性」

Michael mentioned upcoming visits to major Indian brands Boat and Noise. Satish provided a crucial reality check: do not expect traditional OEM design orders from them.

Michael 提及即將拜訪印度大廠 Boat 與 Noise。Satish 提出關鍵事實查核：別期待能從他們手中接到傳統的 OEM 研發訂單。

  • Nature: They are fundamentally "Traders" (Buy & Sell), not R&D-driven design houses. They typically do not have their own designs to outsource for manufacturing.
  • 本質分析： 他們本質上是「貿易商」（買賣導向），而非研發驅動的設計公司。他們通常沒有自己的設計圖可發包給代工廠。
  • Implication: They source finished or semi-finished solutions directly. Simply asking for "manufacturing orders" may lead to a dead end.
  • 戰略意涵： 他們直接採購成品或半成品方案。若我方僅單純尋求「代工訂單」，恐將無功而返。

Item 3: Strategic Pivot - Exclusive Control vs. Cost Efficiency

議題三：戰略轉折 - 從「獨家控貨」到「成本優勢」

Satish's Hypothesis: Monopolize the solution at the China source (Design House) to force Indian brands to work with Foxlink exclusively.

Satish 的假設： 在中國源頭（方案商）壟斷產品方案，迫使印度品牌必須與正崴獨家合作。

Michael's Rebuttal & Pivot: Exclusivity is unrealistic in the fragmented Shenzhen market (3+ competitors per solution). Foxlink will not become a design house. However, we can pivot to a "Cost Reduction" value proposition.

Michael 的反思與轉折： 「獨家」在高度競爭的深圳市場（單一產品至少三家競品）是不切實際的。正崴不會轉型為方案商，但我們可以轉向「降本增效」的價值主張。

The Real Opportunity: Even if Boat/Noise are traders, they act as importers. By shifting their SMT/Assembly to Foxlink India, we can offer significant savings on Import Duties (PCBA vs. Finished Goods) and simplify local certifications (BIS). We don't just sell them capacity; we sell them tariff savings.

真正機會點： 即使 Boat/Noise 是貿易商，他們仍需進口。若能將其貼片/組裝 (SMT/Assembly) 轉移至正崴印度，我們能提供巨大的關稅節省（PCBA vs 成品稅差）及在地認證優勢。我們賣的不是產能，是關稅紅利。

Item 4: Long-term Strategic Task - Upstream Intelligence & Development

議題四：長期戰略任務 - 上游情報佈建與業務開發

Targeting Shenzhen Source: Leverage Foxlink China and Satish's network to broadcast a message to Shenzhen design houses: "If you frequently export PCBA to India, Foxlink India can provide local SMT services."

鎖定深圳源頭： 透過中國 Foxlink 或 Satish 的人脈網絡，在深圳方案商圈釋放消息：「若有頻繁出口 PCBA 至印度的需求，可找正崴印度提供在地 SMT 服務。」

  • Service Offering: Offer either pure SMT (for duty savings) or a full turnkey solution (SMT to FATP), creating a "one-stop" export channel for them.
  • 服務模式： 提供純 SMT 代工（節省關稅）或從 SMT 到 FATP（組裝測試包裝）的一條龍服務，為其打造一站式出口通道。
  • Intelligence Gathering: Utilize upcoming meetings with Boat and Noise to reverse-engineer their supply chain. The goal is to identify which design houses/suppliers they are buying from, then target those upstream players directly.
  • 情報蒐集（反向工程）： 利用對接 Boat、Noise 採購的機會，摸清其背後的供應商/方案商名單。這是一項長期的情報蒐集任務，目標是鎖定真正掌控訂單源頭的方案公司。
