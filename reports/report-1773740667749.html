5. Supply Chain & Distribution Logic

5. 通路與供應鏈佈局邏輯

Ecosystem Whitelisting

生態系白名單機制

  • No Direct Sales: IBSA doesn't buy. They dictate vendor dispatch via "Standard Certification".
  • 不直接採購： IBSA 不直接買貨，而是透過「標準認證」引導採購流向。
  • Fleet Recommendations: Certified vendors are pushed to Zomato, Swiggy, and B2B logistics fleets.
  • 車隊推薦： 通過認證的供應商，會被強烈推薦給 Zomato, Swiggy 等大型配送車隊。

Sourcing Mandate

採購決策權

  • Decentralized Buying: Final call rests with the Sourcing Head of individual member operators.
  • 分散採購： 採購下單權限在各運營商 (如 Battery Smart) 的 Sourcing Head 手中。
  • Compliance is Key: Components failing IBSA/BIS norms face zero dispatch chances as they lose FAME subsidy.
  • 合規為王： 未達標準之組件無法獲得政府補貼，等於無法進入市場。
