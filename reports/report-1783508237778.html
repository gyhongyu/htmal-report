5. Sales & Channel Strategy

5. 銷售與通路佈局

Channel Strategy

通路策略概況

  • Pure B2G Direct Model: Zero reliance on distributors. Heavy dependence on direct Indian Armed Forces procurement & MoD iDEX challenges.
  • 純 B2G/B2B 直銷： 完全不依賴經銷商。高度依賴印度武裝部隊直接採購與國防部 iDEX 標案。
  • No B2C Presence: Tailor-made for tactical defense & heavy logistics. Zero retail consumer focus.
  • 無民生消費市場： 產品針對戰術防禦與高負載物流定制，不針對一般消費者。
  • Digital Footprint: Highly active on LinkedIn/Twitter for investor & Govt PR. No E-commerce sales (Amazon, Moglix, etc.) due to classification.
  • 數位與電商： 基於國防機密，無任何電商佈局。社群經營(LinkedIn)主要面向政府決策者與投資人展示研發實力。

Supply Chain Logistics Logic

供應鏈物流邏輯

  • Closed Loop Network: Highly vertically integrated. Products dispatched directly from Noida plant to military bases via secure channels.
  • 高度垂直整合的封閉網絡： 產品由 Noida 工廠封裝，透過國防專屬物流直接交付軍方。
  • Stringent Vendor Criteria: Strict NDAs, rigorous quality traceability, and absolute stability requirements due to defense classification.
  • 嚴苛供應商門檻： 因國防機密屬性，對供應商保密協議(NDA)、品質溯源及交期穩定性要求極度嚴格。
