5. Sales & Channel Strategy

5. 銷售與通路佈局

Channel Tactics

通路策略概況

  • Distributor-Led + Design-In: Utilizing global giants (WPG, Arrow) & strong local distis (Millennium) while FAEs lead technical wins.
  • 代理商先行與技術支持雙軌： 依賴全球代理(WPG, Arrow)及本土強勢代理(Millennium)，並由原廠 FAE 主導技術。
  • Direct to Giants: Winbond FAEs pitch directly to Jio, Airtel, TVS, Tata. Once spec'd, fulfillment is done via EMS.
  • 大客戶直供： 對接電信巨頭(Jio, Airtel)與車企，確認規格後由代理商配合 EMS 廠拉貨。
  • Digital/B2B Presence: Massive component presence on global platforms (Mouser, DigiKey) and integrated into local disti portals.
  • 數位平台： 於 Mouser, DigiKey 等全球 B2B 平台提供小批量樣品，並整合進本土代理商線上系統。

Logistics Logic

供應鏈物流邏輯

  • Regional Route: Chips shipped from Taiwan/Singapore HQs to APAC hubs, then imported to Mumbai/Chennai ports.
  • 區域發貨： 晶片由台灣/新加坡出貨，進口至孟買、清奈或新德里港口。
  • JIT Fulfillment: Local distributors hold bonded inventory and do Just-In-Time dispatch to EMS giants like Foxlink, Foxconn, and Dixon.
  • JIT 分銷： 由代理商的本地保稅庫分拆，JIT 發貨給 Foxlink (正崴)、富士康、Dixon 等 EMS 廠。
