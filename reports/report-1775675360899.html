2. Discussion Record 貳、 會議討論內容

Item 1: Strategic Context & Mutual Trust

議題一：戰略背景與企業情誼 (Strategic Context & Corporate Ties)

This high-level meeting officially established the substantive framework for Foxlink's "Year One" operational launch in India. Discussions between President Thomas and President Benjamin highlighted the core value of Taiwanese enterprises amid global supply chain restructuring.

本次高層會晤確立了 Foxlink 印度業務「開發元年」的實質性框架。在 Thomas 總裁與 Benjamin 總裁的對話中，雙方確認了台資企業在全球供應鏈重組中的核心價值。

Strategic Implication: Delta’s extensive operational history (entered India in 1993) and their focus on long-term product excellence rather than financial engineering form a strong foundation of mutual trust. In India's complex business ecosystem, this shared cultural alignment serves as a "soft power" that significantly lowers communication costs and accelerates our transition from a mere component supplier to a strategic synergy partner.

戰略意義： Delta 自 1993 年進入印度市場，其「長期耕耘」與「不玩金融遊戲、專注產品」的特質，構建了兩家企業間深厚的互信。在印度的複雜商業生態中，這種基於台資文化的「人情味」與互信不僅是情感紐帶，更是降低溝通成本、加速從單純供應轉向戰略協同的「軟實力」。

Item 2: Delta's Scale, Moat & Manufacturing Excellence

議題二：Delta 營運規模與製造實力分析

Delta has built a highly vertically integrated ecosystem designed to create technical barriers insurmountable by local manufacturers, moving well beyond merely exploiting cheap labor:

Delta 已在印度建立起高度垂直整合的生態體系。其戰略佈局並非僅為了獲取廉價勞動力，而是致力於建構難以被本地廠商跨越的技術門檻：

  • Market Power: Commands a 50-60% market share in Telecom Power, granting them absolute pricing power.
  • 市場滲透： 通訊電源 (Telecom Power) 市佔率高達 50-60%，具備絕對定價權。
  • R&D & Infrastructure: Houses 400-500 R&D engineers in Bangalore. Delta heavily invested millions to secure naming rights for a metro station ("Delta Station"). This is a strategic moat to solve extreme traffic pain points and create a strong "talent retention barrier."
  • 研發與基礎建設： 班加羅爾總部配置約 500 名研發工程師。Delta 更斥資取得地鐵站「Delta Station」命名權，透過解決交通痛點構建高效的「招聘與留才屏障」。
  • De-risking via Automation: Delta's CEO enforces a strict minimum 40% automation rate (factory managers face termination if unmet). This is not to save labor costs, but to ensure "quality stability" and neutralize the uncertainties of India's infrastructure. Combined with 95% in-house tooling/CNC capability, they have completely bypassed local supply chain fragility.
  • 自動化避險策略： Delta 嚴格執行 40% 的自動化率門檻。自動化在印度非為節省人力成本，而是為了確保「質量穩定性」並降低落後成本，抵銷基礎環境不確定性。配合 95% 的工裝與 CNC 自製能力，徹底解決當地供應鏈瓶頸。

Item 3: Technical Synergy & Product Integration (Total Solution)

議題三：核心產品領域與技術協同

Both parties explored actionable pathways to integrate Foxlink components with Delta's systems to provide comprehensive Turnkey Solutions:

雙方探討了透過整合 Foxlink 組件與 Delta 系統，提供「Total Solution」的具體路徑：

1. Electric Vehicles (EV): Delta possesses Powertrain technology (Motor + Controller + Gearbox) but lacks Battery Packs. Foxlink will leverage its extensive battery pack experience (Google, Gogoro, Lyft) to form a Turnkey Solution targeted at the massive Indian 2W/3W market, significantly boosting our joint bargaining power.

1. 電動車 (EV) 領域： Delta 具備動力總成技術（馬達+控制器+齒輪箱），但在電池組 (Battery Pack) 有缺口。Foxlink 將引入在 Google、Gogoro 等專案的電池實戰經驗，結合成一體化解決方案，針對印度兩輪/三輪車市場提供 Turnkey 方案以提升議價權。

2. Data Center & AI: The market is actively shifting from AOC to AEC due to US DSP supply chain limitations. Foxlink's AEC technology can be deeply integrated with Delta's CDU cooling systems, AI Server Power, and Level 13 Rack solutions.

2. 數據中心 (Data Center)： 由於美國 DSP 供應鏈限制，市場正由主動光纜 (AOC) 轉向主動電纜 (AEC)。Foxlink 正開發的 AEC 技術可與 Delta 的 CDU 水冷系統、AI Server Power 及整機櫃解決方案進行深度配套。

3. Energy & Automation: Foxlink's absolute advantage in high-quality industrial cables will directly support Delta's upcoming mass production of PV Inverters, Wind Inverters, and specialized railway PQC products.

3. 能源與工業自動化： Foxlink 在高品質線纜具備絕對優勢，可直接支援 Delta 即將量產的光伏逆變器 (PV Inverter)、風能變流器及鐵路專用產品。

Item 4: Client Dynamics & Market Intelligence

議題四：客戶動態與市場競爭情報

A deep dive was conducted regarding India's extreme procurement culture and infrastructure realities:

會中對印度市場極端的採購風格與基礎設施現狀進行了深度解構：

  • Reliance Industries Risks: Reliance frequently utilizes information asymmetry ("closed-door" isolation tactics with 30-minute decision limits). Furthermore, their funding is opaque and centrally controlled ("Party Central"), meaning projects can halt instantly. Suppliers must deploy highly localized professional teams to manage this extreme pressure.
  • Reliance (信實工業) 風險剖析： 該客戶慣用「關門會議」等資訊不對稱戰術限時決策，且資金鏈受中央管控，極度不透明，專案具「說停就停」特質。供應商必須配置深諳本地文化的專業團隊以應對極限施壓。
  • SpaceX/Starlink Leapfrog Opportunity: Terrestrial optical fiber in India is frequently destroyed by local gangs for extortion or by animals, making maintenance costs astronomical. Consequently, India is leapfrogging wired broadband directly to 5G A-Fiber and LEO satellites. As a component supplier for SpaceX, Foxlink must collaborate with Delta to capture the dividends of this infrastructure shift.
  • SpaceX 與 Starlink 的「跳板戰略」： 印度地面光纖常遭人為破壞勒索，維修成本極高，導致市場將直接跨越有線寬頻，進入 5G 與低軌衛星通訊。Foxlink 作為 SpaceX 之組件供應商，應與 Delta 協同搶佔此基礎設施變更紅利。
