5. 銷售、通路佈局與物流邏輯

100% Direct Custom B2B Model

100% 直銷與深度客製化 B2B 模式

  • Strict AVL Limits: Micropack bypasses standard distributors. Customers must execute intense, multi-stage physical factory audits to list Micropack on their Approved Vendor Lists (AVL).
  • 極高 AVL 准入限制： 無任何零售或經銷代理，不經由第三方。客戶必須通過極其嚴格的實地廠審與准入。
  • R&D Co-Design: Dedicated Field Application Engineers (FAE) coordinate directly with defense and aerospace client R&D teams during initial prototype (Proto-stage) design-in phases.
  • 研發協同設計： 直銷網路由 FAE (現場應用工程師) 負責，在研發階段 (Proto-stage) 即與客戶研發團隊深度協作。
  • Digital Presence: No online e-commerce setup due to military confidentiality. Only maintains basic yellow-page presence on IndiaMART for offline B2B client contact.
  • 數位平台存在感： 基於軍工國防機密，絕無電商零售渠道。在 IndiaMART 等工業 B2B 平台上僅作基礎「黃頁展示」用於線下引流。
  • Official Link: https://www.micropack.in/ - Gateway for clients to download technical compliance certificates (AS9100D, NADCAP, etc.).
  • 官網連結： https://www.micropack.in/ - 主要用於客戶下載 AS9100D、NADCAP 等軍規認證書之官方直連窗口。
