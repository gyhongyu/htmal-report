2. Discussion Record 貳、 會議討論內容

Item 1: Quotation Review Results

議題一：報價審核結果 (Approved)

Eric Wu reviewed Foxlink's preliminary quotation for the automotive electronics project. He confirmed that Foxlink's pricing is highly competitive and the cost structure is superior to existing suppliers. The quote has been validated and approved at the purchasing level.

Eric Wu 詳細審核了 Foxlink 針對車用電子項目提交的粗估報價。其確認 Foxlink 的報價極具競爭力，且成本結構優於現有供應商。目前在價格層面上，已正式通過採購端的內部驗證 (Approved)。

Item 2: Current Supply Chain Structure Analysis

議題二：現行供應鏈結構分析

Analysis of Sensetek/Senvision's supply chain reveals a highly vertical integration with group affiliates:

針對 Sensetek/Senvision 的現行供應鏈運作模式進行梳理，發現其高度依賴集團關聯企業：

  • Key Components (Shine Optics, China): Supplies Sensor, Power IC, Lens, and Serializer. Note: Shine Optics is also a major shareholder/holding entity.
  • 關鍵零組件 (Shine Optics 盛泰, 中國)： 供應 Sensor、Power IC、Lens 等核心元件。備註：盛泰不僅是供應商，更是主要的控股方。
  • Harness (Golden Corn, Thailand): Exclusive supplier for wire harnesses.
  • 線束 (Golden Corn 祥龍, 泰國)： 目前唯一的線束供應來源。
  • Sales Flow: Sensetek (Mfg) -> Senvision (Trading) -> Aisin Haryana -> Maruti Suzuki.
  • 銷售流向： Sensetek (製造) -> Senvision (貿易) -> Aisin Haryana -> Maruti Suzuki。

Item 3: Strategic Barriers to Entry

議題三：供應商導入之結構性壁壘

Despite the pricing advantage, two major structural barriers prevent immediate Official Quotation/Mass Production:

儘管價格具備優勢，但要推進至正式報價 (Official Quotation) 與量產，目前面臨兩大結構性挑戰：

1. Aisin Registration: The BOM is locked with Aisin. Adding Foxlink requires a formal ECN/PCN process and Aisin's approval.

1. Aisin 立項報備限制： BOM 表已鎖定既有供應商。導入 Foxlink 需啟動正式的 ECN/PCN 變更流程 並獲得 Aisin 核准。

2. Holding Company Interest: Decisions are driven by Shine Optics/Sensetek holding executives. Replacing group companies with Foxlink requires strong justification beyond price, specifically regarding "Track Record" and risk management.

2. 控股高層決策權： 決策核心在於 Shine Optics (盛泰) 與 Sensetek 控股高層。由於涉及集團內部利益，僅憑價格難以說服高層承擔更換供應商的風險 (缺乏 Track Record)。

Item 4: Breakthrough Strategy - Executive Engagement

議題四：突破策略 - 決策核心拜訪

To bypass local limitations, it was decided that Foxlink must engage directly with the decision-makers at the holding level (Sensetek/Shine Optics). The goal is to present Foxlink's global automotive capabilities (e.g., Tesla case study) to mitigate the "New Supplier" risk and secure a top-down directive.

為突破現有層級的限制，會議決議 Foxlink 必須「越級」直接拜訪 Sensetek 與 Shine Optics 的控股決策者。目標是透過展示 Foxlink 的全球車載實績（如 Tesla 案例），消除高層對新供應商的疑慮，進而取得「由上而下」的推動力。
