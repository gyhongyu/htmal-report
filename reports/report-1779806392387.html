5. Procurement Channel Strategy

5. 採購決策與供應鏈網絡分析

B2B Procurement Model

採購與通路模式 (B2B)

  • Centralized Setup: Core servers & software licenses are driven by Global Master Agreements signed by US HQ with OEMs.
  • 集中化採購： 核心伺服器與大型軟體授權，由美國總部與全球 OEM 簽訂框架協議 (Master Agreements)。
  • Localized Deployment: Gurugram procurement holds high autonomy for endpoint devices (headsets, keyboards, UPS, network cabling), preferring local System Integrators (SIs).
  • 在地化佈建採購： 針對終端設備 (耳機、UPS、網路佈線)，Gurugram 團隊擁有極高決策權，傾向與印度本地系統整合商及 OEM 分公司合作。

Supply Chain & Logistics Logic

供應鏈物流邏輯

  • Pan-India Capability: Vendors must offer Pan-India fulfillment and warranty support.
  • 泛印度交付能力： 供應商需具備「泛印度 (Pan-India) 交付與保固能力」。
  • OEM Integration: If Foxlink sells to HP/Dell to win Concentrix, Foxlink must ensure steady component supply to OEM assembly plants in Tamil Nadu or UP.
  • OEM 供應整合： 若透過 HP/Dell 拿單，Foxlink 需確保能將關鍵組件穩定供貨至這些 OEM 在 Tamil Nadu 或 UP 邦的本地組裝廠。
