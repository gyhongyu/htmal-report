3.2. Core Need: Type-C Charging Cable 3.2. 核心需求：Type-C 充電線

The client's immediate requirement is for a Type-C charging cable, which will be included as a standard accessory with their new hardware product.

客戶的立即需求是 Type-C 充電線。此線纜將作為其新硬體產品的標準配件，一同包裝在產品盒內（非單獨銷售）。

  • Usage: Bundled in the box with the main device and its charging case.
  • Required Quantity: 500,000 pcs for the first order, with an estimated annual quantity of 800,000 pcs.
  • Key Specification: Must support 60W (20V/3A) charging speed.
  • Customization: The client requires their brand name/logo to be printed on the cable.
  • 產品用途： 與主機、充電盒一同包裝在盒內 (in the box)。
  • 需求數量： 首單 500,000 條，預估年用量 (Annual Quantity) 約 800,000 條。
  • 關鍵規格： 需支援 60W (20V/3A) 的充電速度。
  • 客製化： 客戶要求在線纜上客製化印刷品牌名稱 (customized name)。
3.3. Client Product: "Portable Health Monitoring Device" 3.3. 客戶產品："便攜式健康監測裝置"

The client introduced their flagship product, a high-tech wearable health monitor. This is the device the charging cable is intended for.

客戶介紹了他們正在開發的旗艦產品，是一款高科技的可攜式健康監測裝置，此充電線即為該產品使用。

  • Product Name: 'Portable health monitoring device' (Confirmed via WhatsApp for reporting purposes).
  • Functionality: This advanced device not only tracks cardiovascular status (like Apple Watch) but also monitors "brain health" and "brain flow".
  • Development Status: Currently in the prototype stage. The final device casing is planned to use Aluminium and Titanium.
  • 產品名稱： ‘Portable health monitoring device’ (經 WhatsApp 確認，用於報告的正式名稱)。
  • 產品功能： 此高階裝置不僅能監測心血管狀態（類似 Apple Watch），更能監測「腦部健康 (brain health)」與「腦血流 (brain flow)」。
  • 開發狀態： 目前為原型機 (Prototype) 階段。最終產品外殼計畫使用鋁合金 (Aluminium) 和鈦金屬 (Titanium)。
