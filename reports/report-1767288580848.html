2. Discussion Record 貳、 會議討論內容

Item 1: Structural Integration of Mickeyfone & Wangda

議題一：Mickeyfone 與王大科技的架構整合

Context: To evaluate the operational efficiency and division of labor between the two entities for potential subcontracting.

背景： 評估兩家實體間的營運效率與分工，以利後續外包業務規劃。

Detail: Confirmed both companies share the same management. Mickeyfone acts as the primary EMS window for Reliance (Jio) routers and STBs, while Wangda specializes in mechanical engineering, including molding and blister packaging.

細節： 確認兩家公司由同一團隊管理。Mickeyfone 是 Reliance (Jio) 網通設備的主代工窗口，而王大則專精於機構工程，包含注塑與吸塑包裝。

Implication: Foxlink can leverage Wangda's localized blister production (2-week sampling, 8% duty saving) to reduce overall FATP costs while focusing on high-level system integration.

影響： 正崴可利用王大在地的吸塑產線（2 週出樣、節省 8% 關稅）降低整體組裝成本，讓正崴專注於高價值的系統整合。

Item 2: Router Market Dynamics & Reliance (Jio) Potential

議題二：路由器市場動態與 Reliance (Jio) 商機

Context: Analyzing entry barriers and growth potential in the 5G infrastructure market.

背景： 分析 5G 基礎設施市場的進入門檻與增長潛力。

Detail: Morton highlighted that the market is in a "Redividend Period" due to Jio's massive 5G FWA/FTTH rollout. Key barriers are SDK software integration and manufacturing yield. Currently, India lacks high-precision hardware and specialized chemicals.

細節： Morton 強調由於 Jio 大規模推動 5G FWA/FTTH，市場正處於紅利爆發期。關鍵門檻在於 SDK 軟體整合與製程良率。目前印度在地仍缺乏高精度五金及特殊化學材料。

Implication: Foxlink’s capability to supply these critical components and materials fills a significant supply chain gap, positioning us as a preferred strategic provider for upcoming Jio projects.

影響： 正崴供應關鍵零件與材料的能力彌補了印度供應鏈的顯著缺口，使我們成為 Jio 未來專案的首選戰略供應商。

Item 3: Synergy Model & North-South Capacity Linking

議題三：協作模式與南北產能聯動計畫

Context: Moving beyond simple OEM to a comprehensive strategic partnership.

背景： 從單純的代工關係轉向全面的戰略合作夥伴關係。

Detail: Discussed leveraging Mickeyfone’s 3 idle SMT lines in Chennai to support overflow from Foxlink’s Tirupati plant. Foxlink provides the "Ecosystem" (AI/Connectors/Global R&D), while they provide "Lean Local Manufacturing."

細節： 討論利用 Mickeyfone 在清奈閒置的 3 條 SMT 線，支援正崴提魯帕蒂廠的溢出訂單。正崴提供「全領域生態」（AI/連接器/全球研發），而對方提供「在地精悍製造」。

Implication: This regional synergy solves immediate capacity constraints and builds a multi-node manufacturing network in India to serve Tier-1 customers more flexibly.

影響： 此區域協作解決了眼前的產能限制，並在印度構建了多節點製造網絡，能更靈活地服務一級客戶。
